#### Skills
*(Innate)* **Promethean Vision:** The Crawler (Alpha) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness, and can see any creature within 60 feet that is not obscured by cover.

*(Innate)* **Headshot Susceptibility:** Any headshot scored against the Crawler (Alpha) deals +1 additional damage die.

*(Innate)* **Nimble:** The Crawler (Alpha) can take the Dash maneuver using no Operations.

*(Passive)* **Leader of the Pack:** Any Crawler that is aware of the Crawler (Alpha)’s presence within 40 feet is immune to the Frightened effect and gains an additional Secondary and Tertiary Operation.

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Unique Weapon:** The Hunter uses the Assault Cannon attached to its arm to make all weapon attacks.

_(Innate)_ **Deployed in Pairs:** They are never alone, as they are always deployed in sets of two.

_(Innate)_ **Rage:** When one Hunter is killed and its partner is still alive, the remaining Hunter gains the following enhancements; Receives +100 health. Dodge bonus increases to +5. Movement speed increases by +10 feet. Whenever the Hunter makes an attack with its Assault Cannon, it can make a melee attack as a Secondary Operation.

_(Passive)_ **Shielded:** The Hunter has a +20 to all dodges from attackers that it can see. If the attack would otherwise hit, it hits the shield. The shield has 500 health.

_(Passive)_ **Armored:** All damage dealt to the Hunter is halved unless the hunter fails the dodge by 40 or more, is dealt explosive damage or is exposed to the Surprised Effect.

#### Special Abilities:
**Detonation:** The target creature must make a dodge or take full damage, taking half damage (rounded down) on a success. Any creature within 5 feet of the detonation must make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

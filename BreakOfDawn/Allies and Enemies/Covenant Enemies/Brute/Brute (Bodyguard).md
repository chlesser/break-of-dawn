#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Charge:** As a Secondary and Tertiary Operations the Brute (Bodyguard) can move half of their movement speed (rounded down) and gain an additional +15 Dodge bonus against Chance Attacks.

_(Innate)_ **Grab:** If the Brute (Bodyguard) hits a melee attack, they may immediately take the Grapple combat maneuver, expending only a Secondary and Tertiary Operation.

_(Innate)_ **Frenzy:** When the Brute (Bodyguard) reaches 30 health or lower, they take only half damage (rounded down) from Kinetic, Hardlight, and Plasma damage, and their melee damage increases by +9 (2d8) damage die.

_(Passive)_ **Ward:** Each Brute (Bodyguard) has one creature it is chosen to protect. For every Brute (Bodyguard) protecting that creature, that creature subtracts -5 (1d8) damage from each incoming attack.

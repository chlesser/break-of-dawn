#### Skills

_(Innate)_ **Charge:** As a Secondary and Tertiary Operations the Brute (Ultra) can move half of their movement speed (rounded down) and gain an additional +15 Dodge bonus against Chance Attacks.

_(Innate)_ **Grab:** If the Brute (Ultra) hits a melee attack, they may immediately take the Grapple combat maneuver, expending only a Secondary and Tertiary Operation.

_(Innate)_ **Frenzy:** When the Brute (Ultra) reaches 25 health or lower, they take only half damage (rounded down) from Kinetic, Hardlight, and Plasma damage, and their melee damage increases by +9 (2d8) damage die.

_(Passive)_ **Brutal Attacks:** The Brute (Ultra) may reroll 1s on any damage die, but they must use the new roll regardless of whether it is higher or not.

#### Skills

_(Innate)_ **Charge:** As a Secondary and Tertiary Operations the Brute (Chieftain) can move half of their movement speed (rounded down) and gain an additional +15 Dodge bonus against Chance Attacks.

_(Innate)_ **Grab:** If the Brute (Chieftain) hits a melee attack, they may immediately take the Grapple combat maneuver, expending only a Secondary and Tertiary Operation.

_(Innate)_ **Frenzy:** When the Brute (Chieftain) reaches 70 health or lower, they take only half damage (rounded down) from Kinetic, Hardlight, and Plasma damage, and their melee damage increases by +14 (3d8) damage die.

_(Passive)_ **Alpha:** Whenever the Brute (Chieftain) hits an attack they choose 1 ally and that ally deals +1 additional damage die on their next attack.

_(Passive)_ **Vicious:** If the Brute (Chieftain) deals more than 60 damage in a turn to a single target, that creature is exposed to the Wounded effect until they regain health in any way or complete a downtime.

_(Passive)_ **Menacing:** Enemy creatures within 20 feet of the Brute (Chieftain) must make a DC 55 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Frightened effect. Each creature affected by this can remake the Fortitude interaction roll at the end of each turn. A creature that succeeds its interaction roll becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.

_(Passive)_ **Unwavering Presence:** Any allies that can see the Brute (Chieftain) are immune to the Frightened effect.

_(Passive)_ **Deadly Attacks:** When their attack hits, the Brute (Chieftain) may roll for damage twice and take the higher result.

_(Passive)_ **No Weaklings on my Watch:** If an allied Brute within 20 feet of the Brute (Chieftain) is reduced to 0 health, the Brute (Chieftain) may choose to restore them to 1 health. An ally may not benefit from this ability more than once.

_(Operational)_ **Brutal Resilience:** Once, as a Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary Operation, the Brute (Chieftain) can regain 46 (8d10) health.

_(Operational)_ **Resist:** As a reactionary Operation, after being hit by an attack the Brute (Chieftain) can roll 1d12 and subtract the result from the damage taken.

_(Operational)_ **Callout:** Once per round, as a Secondary Operation, the Brute (Chieftain) may choose 1 ally that it can see within 60 feet. The next time that ally makes an attack, they may roll 11 (2d10) and increase the Dodge DC of their attack by the amount rolled on the die.

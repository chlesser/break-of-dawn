#### Skills
_(Passive)_ **Overshield:** All allies have their maximum shield capacity double while within 50 feet of the Huragok, or if they do not have shields, they instead receive maximum shields equal to their base maximum health while in range. These additional shields are removed when the Huragok dies.

_(Passive)_ **Suicide:** If the Huragok falls below 20 health but is not killed, on its next turn it will use all of its movement towards the nearest target and explode, whether or not it reaches the target. If the target is within 5 feet, it must make a Dodge DC 70 or take 28 (5d10) Explosive damage, or half as much on a success (rounded down). Any other creatures within 5 feet of the Huragok must also make a Dodge DC 70. On a failure, they take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

_(Operational)_ **Disable:** As a Primary Operation, the Huragok can temporarily render 1 electronic device or weapon that it can touch unusable for up to 1 hour.

_(Operational)_ **Protector:** The Huragok can end 1 effect that isn’t the Wounded effect on an ally it can touch as a Secondary Operation.

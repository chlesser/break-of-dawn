#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Drone (Minor) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Hive:** When a Drone within 10 feet of the Drone (Minor) that it can see makes an attack against a target visible to the Drone (Minor) within range, the Drone (Minor) can immediately make a single attack against the same target.

_(Passive)_ **Weapon Inefficiency:** The maximum amount of weapon attacks the Drone (Minor) can make in a turn is halved (rounded down).

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Drone (Captain Major) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Hive:** When a Drone within 10 feet of the Drone (Captain Major) that it can see makes an attack against a target visible to the Drone (Captain Major) within range, the Drone (Captain Major) can immediately make a single attack against the same target.

_(Passive)_ **Swarm:** As a Primary Operation the Drone (Captain Major) can force as many drones as it chooses within 50 feet to move a distance up to their maximum movement speed closer to the Drone (Captain Major).

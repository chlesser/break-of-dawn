#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Drone (Ultra) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Hive:** When a Drone within 10 feet of the Drone (Ultra) that it can see makes an attack against a target visible to the Drone (Ultra) within range, the Drone (Ultra) can immediately make a single attack against the same target.

_(Passive)_ **Battlefield Instinct:** If the Drone (Ultra) rolls a 5 or lower on any dodge, it may reroll but must use the new roll.

_(Passive)_ **Sneak Attack:** When the Drone (Ultra) attacks a target that is exposed to the Surprised effect, the attack deals +1 additional damage die.

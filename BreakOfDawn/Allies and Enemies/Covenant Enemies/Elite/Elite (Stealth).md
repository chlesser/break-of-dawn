#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Stealth) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Stealth) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 50, the Elite (Stealth) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Stealth), the Elite (Stealth) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Assassinate:** Any attack hit on a creature exposed to the Surprised effect deals an additional +28 (5d10) damage die.

_(Operational)_ **Enhanced Invisibility:** The Elite (Stealth) can take a Primary Operation to turn invisible for up to a minute no more than once per minute, exposing them to the Invisible effect, however instead of a +20 bonus to dodges and Stealth interactions, they have a +30 bonus.

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness. 

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20. 

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Silent Shadow Commander), the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight. 

_(Passive)_ **Assassinate:** Any attack hit on a creature exposed to the Surprised effect deals an additional +55 (10d10) damage die. _(Passive)_ **Unique Weapons:** Energy swords that the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) wields are blood red instead of the standard light blue. 

_(Operational)_ **Invisibility:** As a Primary Operation, the Elite (Silent Shadow) can turn invisible for up to 1 minute, no more than once per minute, exposing them to the Invisible effect. 

_(Operational)_ **Coordinated Attack:** As a Secondary Operation the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander) can choose 1 Elite (Silent Shadow) that it can see within 50 feet to make an attack at the same time as the Elite (Silent Shadow Commander).

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Honor Guard) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Honor Guard) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Honor Guard) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Honor Guard), the Elite (Honor Guard) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Defender:** While within 10 feet of at least 1 other Elite (Honor Guard) or (Light of Sanghelios), the Elite (Honor Guard) regains 10 shields at the start of each of its turns, but cannot exceed its maximum shields.

_(Passive)_ **Honorbound:** If the item or person that the Elite (Honor Guard) is protecting is destroyed or killed, all remaining Elite (Honor Guards) gain the Indomitable effect for 1 hour or until they die.

_(Operational)_ **Protector:** The Elite (Honor Guard) can end 1 effect that isn’t the Wounded effect on an ally it can touch as a Secondary Operation.

_(Operational)_ **Sacrifice:** If an ally within 10 feet of the Elite (Honor Guard) is hit by an attack, the Elite (Honor Guard) can choose to make themselves the target of the attack instead as a Reactive Operation, suffering full effects.

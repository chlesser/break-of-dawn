#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Spec Ops Commander) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Spec Ops Commander) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Spec Ops Commander) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Spec Ops Commander), the Elite (Spec Ops Commander) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Leader:** When 3 or more Spec Ops of any race are conscious within 50 feet of the Elite (Spec Ops Commander), the Elite (Spec Ops Commander) gains an additional +10 dodge and may make an additional attack with their weapon.

_(Operational)_ **Invisibility:** As a Primary Operation, the Elite (Spec Ops) can turn invisible for up to 1 minute, no more than once per minute, exposing them to the Invisible effect.

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (General) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (General) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 60, the Elite (General) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (General), the Elite (General) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Commanding Presence:** Allies within 15 feet gain an additional +5 Dodge Bonus, excluding the Elite (General) itself.

_(Operational)_ **Tactical Expert:** The Elite (General) can make a Tactics check against 1 target it can see within 50 feet as a Secondary Operation. The target must then make a Charisma or Tactics interaction (target’s choice) and if the Elite (General)’s roll is higher, then it successfully predicts the movements of its targets and any attacks made against that target during the current turn have an additional +10 to their Dodge DC.

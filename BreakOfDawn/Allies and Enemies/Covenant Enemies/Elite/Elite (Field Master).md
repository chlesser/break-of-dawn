#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Field Master) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Field Master) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Field Master) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Field Master), the Elite (Field Master) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Battle Tactics:** On their turn, the Elite (Field Master) can sacrifice up to half their movement speed (rounded down) in feet and choose an allied creature that can see and hear them. On the target creature’s next turn, their movement speed is increased by the amount of feet sacrificed by the Elite (Field Master) until the end of their turn.

_(Passive)_ **Inspiring Presence:** Any allies within 15 feet of the Elite (Field Master) are immune to all negative effects presented by any battlefield externalities.

_(Passive)_ **Menacing:** Enemy creatures within 20 feet of the Elite (Field Master) must make a DC 50 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Frightened effect. Each creature affected by this can remake the Fortitude interaction roll at the end of each turn. A creature that succeeds its interaction roll becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.

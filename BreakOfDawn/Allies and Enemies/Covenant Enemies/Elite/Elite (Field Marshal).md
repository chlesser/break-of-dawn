#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Zealot) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Zealot) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Zealot) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Zealot), the Elite (Zealot) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Leader:** When 3 or more Elites are conscious within 50 feet of the Elite (Field Marshal), the Elite (Field Marshal) gain an additional +10 dodge and may make an additional attack with their weapon.

_(Operational)_ **Armor Lock:** Once per downtime, as a Primary Operation, the Elite (Field Marshal) may enter into an armor lock where they stay for up to 2 turns or until the Elite (Field Marshal) ends it as a Secondary Operation. During this time it suffers a -50 to all dodges and the Elite (Field Marshal)’s speed is reduced to 0 feet, but is immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When the Elite (Field Marshal) exits armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage the Elite (Zealot) took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.

_(Operational)_ **Command:** As a Secondary Operation, the Field Marshal can command up to 1 Elite that is conscious and aware of the Elite (Field Marshal) within 50 feet to make a single attack with a weapon of large size or smaller against 1 creature within their range.

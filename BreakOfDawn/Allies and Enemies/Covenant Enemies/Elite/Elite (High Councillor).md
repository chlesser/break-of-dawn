#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (High Councillor) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (High Councillor) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (High Councillor) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (High Councillor), the Elite (High Councillor) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Embolden:** All allies within 30 feet of the Elite (High Councillor) gain 1 of the following benefits chosen by the Elite (High Councillor), which lasts until the start of the Elite (High Councillor)’s next turn: Regain up to 10 health on the start of their turn; May make an extra attack with a weapon of large size or smaller or melee on their turn; Add +15 to all dodge rolls.

_(Operational)_ **Misdirection:** The Elite (High Councillor) can make a DC 80 Charisma check against 1 enemy that can see and hear it. If the check succeeds, then the target’s attacks have their Dodge DC reduced by -10 until the start of the Elite (High Councillor)’s next turn.

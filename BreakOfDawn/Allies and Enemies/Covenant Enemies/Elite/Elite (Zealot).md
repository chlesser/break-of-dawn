#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Zealot) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Zealot) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 50, the Elite (Zealot) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Zealot), the Elite (Zealot) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Zealous Devotion:** Once per turn, when making an attack, the Elite (Zealot) can sacrifice their +10 Dodge Bonus to impose a -10 Dodge Penalty on their target, but lose their Dodge Bonus until the start of their next turn.

_(Passive)_ **Menacing:** Enemy creatures within 20 feet of the Elite (Zealot) must make a DC 45 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Frightened effect. Each creature affected by this can remake the Fortitude interaction roll at the end of each turn. A creature that succeeds its interaction roll becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.

_(Operational)_ **Armor Lock:** Once per downtime, as a Primary Operation, the Elite (Zealot) may enter into an armor lock where they stay for up to 2 turns or until the Elite (Zealot) ends it as a Secondary Operation. During this time it suffers a -50 to all dodges and the Elite (Zealot)’s speed is reduced to 0 feet, but is immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When the Elite (Field Marshal) exits armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage the Elite (Zealot) took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.

_(Operational)_ **Sacrifice:** If an ally within 10 feet of the Elite (Zealot) is hit by an attack, the Elite (Zealot) can choose to make themselves the target of the attack instead as a Reactive Operation, suffering full damage and effects.

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Imperial Admiral) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Imperial Admiral) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Imperial Admiral) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Imperial Admiral), the Elite (Imperial Admiral) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Leader:** When three or more Elites are conscious within 50 feet of the Elite (Imperial Admiral), the Elite (Imperial Admiral) gains an additional +10 dodge and may make an additional attack with their weapon.

_(Passive)_ **Squadron Leader:** Any vehicles piloted by allies within 100 feet gain the following benefits: Add +10 to all dodges. Gain +30 feet movement speed. The DC for Agility checks required to hijack increases by 20.

_(Passive)_ **Inspiring Presence:** Any allies within 15 feet of the Elite (Imperial Admiral) are immune to all negative effects presented by any battlefield externalities.

_(Passive)_ **Unwavering Presence:** Any allies that can see the Elite (Imperial Admiral) are immune to the Frightened effect.

_(Passive)_ **Menacing:** Enemy creatures within 20 feet of the Elite (Imperial Admiral) must make a DC 55 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Frightened effect. Each creature affected by this can remake the Fortitude interaction roll at the end of each turn. A creature that succeeds its interaction roll becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.

_(Operational)_ **Battle Command:** An Elite (Imperial Admiral) can, as a Secondary Operation, choose an ally within 50 feet who is aware of the Elite (Imperial Admiral). The chosen ally can do 1 of the following during the Elite (Imperial Admiral)’s current turn: Move up to 35 feet in a direction of the Elite (Imperial Admiral)’s choosing; Make a single attack against a target of the Elite (Imperial Admiral)’s choice; Sacrifice up to 125 points of shield health to restore the Elite (Imperial Admiral)’s shields by an equal amount.

#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Ranger Commander) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Ranger Commander) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Ranger Commander) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Ranger Commander), the Elite (Ranger Commander) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Space Effectiveness:** The Elite (Ranger Commander) can ignore movement speed penalties while in space.

_(Passive)_ **Leader:** When 3 or more Rangers of any race are conscious within 50 feet of the Elite (Ranger Commander), the Elite (Ranger Commander) gains an additional +10 dodge and may make an additional attack with their weapon on their turn.

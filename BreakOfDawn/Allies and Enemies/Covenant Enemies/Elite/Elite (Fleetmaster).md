#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Fleetmaster) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Fleetmaster) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Fleetmaster) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Fleetmaster), the Elite (Fleetmaster) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Squadron Leader:** Any vehicles piloted by allies within 100 feet gain the following benefits: Add +15 to all dodges. Gain +40 feet movement speed. The DC for Agility interactions required to hijack increases by 30.

_(Passive)_ **Flagship of the Fleet:** Whenever the Elite (Fleetmaster) is onboard a ship under their command they gain the following benefits: They are immune to the Surprised and Frightened effects. They are exposed to the Indomitable effect. Creatures within sight of the Elite (Fleetmaster) are immune to the Surprised and Frightened effects.

_(Operational)_ **Battle Command:** An Elite (Fleetmaster) can, as a Secondary Operation, choose an ally within 50 feet who is aware of the Elite (Fleetmaster). The chosen ally can do one of the following during the Elite (Fleetmaster)’s current turn: Move up to 25 feet in a direction of the Elite (Fleetmaster)’s choosing; Make a single attack against a target of the Elite (Fleetmaster)’s choice; Sacrifice up to 100 points of shield health to restore the Elite (Fleetmaster)’s shields by an equal amount.

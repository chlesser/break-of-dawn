#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Light of Sanghelios) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 40, the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Light of Sanghelios), the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Defender:** While within 10 feet of at least 1 other Elite (Honor Guard) or (Light of Sanghelios), the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) regains 10 shields at the start of each of its turns, but cannot exceed its maximum shields.

_(Passive)_ **Honorbound:** If the item or person that the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) is protecting is destroyed or killed, all remaining Elite (Light of Sanghelios) gain the Indomitable effect for 1 hour or until they die.

_(Passive)_ **Stunning Strike:** If a creature fails a dodge of one of the Elite (Light of Sanghelios’) attacks, by 35 or more, it is exposed to the Stunned effect until the end of its next turn.

_(Passive)_ **Menacing:** Enemy creatures within 20 feet of the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) must make a DC 50 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Frightened effect. Each creature affected by this can remake the Fortitude interaction roll at the end of each turn. A creature that succeeds its interaction roll becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.

_(Operational)_ **Protector:** The Elite (Light of Sanghelios) can end 1 effect that isn’t the Wounded effect on an ally it can touch as a Secondary Operation.

_(Operational)_ **Sacrifice:** If an ally within 10 feet of the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) is hit by an attack, the Elite (Light of Sanghelios) can choose to make themselves the target of the attack instead as a Reactive Operation, suffering full effects.

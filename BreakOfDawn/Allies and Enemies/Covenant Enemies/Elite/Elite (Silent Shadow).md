#### Skills
_(Innate)_ **Nightvision:** The Elite (Silent Shadow) ignores penalties resulting from Darkness.

_(Innate)_ **Honor In Death:** When the Elite (Silent Shadow) is reduced to 0 health, it may roll a d100. If the result of this roll is above 50, the Elite (Silent Shadow) is not killed and drops to 1 health. Each time it uses this ability the DC increases by 20.

_(Innate)_ **Brotherhood of Warriors:** If an Elite is killed within sight of an Elite (Silent Shadow), the Elite (Silent Shadow) may immediately make a single attack targeted on the creature that killed the Elite, provided they are within range and within line of sight.

_(Passive)_ **Assassinate:** Any attack hit on a creature exposed to the Surprised effect deals an additional +44 (8d10) damage die.

_(Passive)_ **Unique Weapons:** Energy swords that the Elite (Silent Shadow) wields are blood red instead of the standard light blue.

_(Operational)_ **Invisibility:** As a Primary Operation, the Elite (Silent Shadow) can turn invisible for up to 1 minute, no more than once per minute, exposing them to the Invisible effect.

#### Skills
*(Innate)* **Headshot Susceptibility:** Any headshot scored against the Jackal (Sniper) deals +1 additional damage die.

*(Passive)* **Legendary Aim:** When a Jackal (Sniper) makes a successful called shot to the Head of a creature, the attack deals \+2 additional damage die, and the target must make a DC 60 Fortitude interaction or be exposed to the Stunned effect until the end of the target’s next turn.

## ALLY AND ENEMY STATISTICS

In your missions as a fireteam, you will likely encounter many creatures, both hostile and friendly. Some will aid you in battle, but most will stand in the way to your objective. The statistics for these creatures is listed below.

Creature Sizes  
Small: Smaller than 5x5 feet  
Medium: 5x5 feet  
Large: 10x10 feet  
Massive: 15x15 feet or larger

These sizes are accounting for a grid where each square is 5x5 feet.

Statistics Guide

**Combat Maneuvers**  
Unless otherwise stated in the creature’s stat block, creatures are not capable of using any combat maneuvers.

**Weapon Superiority and Expertise**  
Many creatures have superiority or expertise in certain weapons and will thus be significantly more efficient with them. The bonuses they get are dependent on the size of the weapon and are as follows:

**Weapon Superiority**  
Small/Medium: \+1 damage die, \+5 Dodge DC  
Large/Massive: \+2 damage die, \+10 Dodge DC

**Weapon Expertise**  
Small/Medium: \+3 damage die, \+10 Dodge DC  
Large/Massive: \+4 damage die, \+20 Dodge DC

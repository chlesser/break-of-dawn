## Leader
As explosions sound in the distance, a Spartan rises from her trench and leads a charge into a Promethean base, a squad of marines behind her. She runs forward, firing her Battle Rifle with deadly precision, her spartans working in unison to overpower the enemy in moments. Receiving the next objective from her superior, she prepares to earn her squad to yet another victory. She is a leader, master tactician skilled in both offense and defense. Leaders are the driving force behind every fireteam. Other Spartans might have opinions on what to do, but the Leader calls the shots.

**Statistics:**   
Health: 35  
Shields: 50  
Health per rank up: 5 (1d8)  
Speed: 30 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: Battle Rifle, Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Firepower, History  
Expertises: Tactics, Charisma  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 3 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 6 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Command*. Twice per downtime, as a Secondary Operation, you may command an ally to take a Primary Operation at the same time that you take yours. 

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+7 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+4 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Well-Rounded:** You may add \+2 damage to all weapon attacks.  
**Fast:** Gain an extra \+5 feet of movement speed.  
**Medic:** During a downtime, you can create up to 3 medpacs.

### Captain
**Assist:** When someone attacks a creature you damaged during your last turn, each of your allies attacks deals an additional \+3 damage, and the target’s dodge is decreased by \-5.  
**Versatile:** Add \+10 to 2 interactions of your choice.  
**Stalwart:** You have a \+20 to interactions and dodges made to avoid the Frightened and Surprised effects.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+10 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+7 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Flank:** When making an attack against an enemy that did not make an attack against you on their previous turn, they have a \-5 to dodges and you deal an additional 1d4 damage of that weapon’s damage type per attack.  
**Flash Mines:** You can deploy 3 small flash mines per downtime, and each can be placed as a Secondary Operation. They can be stuck onto surfaces, buried, or thrown up to 20 feet, and can be detonated as a Tertiary Operation. Any creature within 5 feet of the mine must succeed on a DC 50 dodge or be exposed to the Deafened and Blinded effects until the end of your next turn.  
**Pilot:** You become much more efficient at using vehicles. Therefore, when in the driver seat of a vehicle, moving the vehicle becomes no Operation, using the Ram ability becomes a Tertiary Operation, and using the vehicle’s weapons becomes a Secondary Operation.

### Colonel Major
**Inspirational Command:** 4 times per downtime, as a Reactive Operation, you may utter a command to a friendly creature other than yourself that can understand you that just made any d100 roll. The creature may now add the result of a d20 roll to their d100 roll..  
**Regeneration Field:** Twice per downtime as a Primary Operation you can throw down a regeneration field with a 10 foot radius. Any creatures of your choice within the field regenerate up to 15 health per turn. This field lasts for up to five turns or until you deactivate it as a Secondary Operation.  
**Relentless Command:** You may use your *Command* ability 3 times per downtime.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+18 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+14 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Armor Lock:**  As a Primary Operation, you may enter into an armor lock where you stay for up to 2 turns or until you end it as a Secondary Operation. During this time you suffer a \-50 to all dodges and have your speed reduced to 0 feet, but you are immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When you exit armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage you took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge. This can be done up to twice per downtime.  
**Wrist Rocket:** As a modification to your armor, you may add a small armament of miniature rockets into your wrist plating. The attachment is capable of holding up to 5 rockets at a time, regaining all rockets during downtime. As a Primary Operation, you can fire as many of these rockets at a target as you wish, with each rocket dealing 4d4 explosive damage with a Dodge DC 80\. This attack can be split between multiple targets. For example, if you fire 5 rockets, you can fire 2 at one enemy and 3 on another, or any other combination of your choice.  
**Ace:** Pick 2 interactions from which you already have expertise/superiority, you gain a \+25 to these interactions. 

### General Major
**Improved Command:** When using your *Command* ability, you can now target up to 2 allies at a time. Additionally, chosen creatures regain \+20 health and gain an additional \+10 movement speed on their turn.  
**Rallying Cry:** Twice per downtime as Primary Operation you can yell a rallying cry. When used, you become immune to all damage for 1 round (within reason, which is to say the GM’s discretion), and up to 3 allies within 30 feet of you are exposed to the Indomitable effect until the end of their next turn.  
**Target:** As a Secondary Operation you may target one creature of your choice, and all attacks against that target do an additional 1d6 damage, and the Dodge DC of these attacks increases by \+5. You may switch which creature is being targeted as a Secondary Operation. 

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+35 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+25 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Master of the Many:** You gain a \+15 to all ability checks you do not have superiority or expertise in.

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**I Like You Guys…:** Your confidence on the battlefield stirs the hearts of your companions. Any allies within 30 feet of you gain one of the following effects while in combat, chosen by you at the start of each of your turns:  
\-You regain \+10 health per turn  
\-You gain \+15 feet of movement speed  
\-You gain \+15 to all dodges  
\-You gain \+5 (1d8) to all weapon attacks  
**But I Don’t Like Them:** Conversely, your confidence on the battlefield strikes fear into the heart of your enemies. Any hostile creatures within 30 feet of you suffer from one of the following effects while in combat, chosen by you at the start of each of your turns:  
\-DC 25 dodge or be stunned  
\-Subtract 10 from their dodges  
\-Subtract 5 (1d8) from all weapon attacks  
\-Subtract 10 feet of movement speed

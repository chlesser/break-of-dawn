## Tracker
Scouting ahead of the rest of the fireteam, the Tracker aims to gather valuable information as to their enemies’ position. Able to sniff out anything without making a sound, she sees through the Covenant raiding party’s camouflage. Quietly coming from behind with a silenced DMR, she takes out the sentries as she aims her final shot into the head of their leader.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 30  
Shields: 40  
Health per rank up: 4 (1d6)  
Speed: 40 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: DMR, Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Medicinal, Detection  
Expertises: Tracking, Stealth  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 2 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 5 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Scout.* Your armor is infused with a small active camo module. Once per downtime, as a Primary Operation, you can become exposed to the Invisible effect, which lasts for up to 10 minutes, or until you deactivate it as a Secondary Operation, make a weapon attack or make a combat maneuver.

### 2nd Lieutenant:  
**Hearty:** Add \+5 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+3 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant:  
**Fast:** Gain an extra \+5 movement speed.  
**Suppressor:** Ranged weapon attacks you make cannot be heard by creatures further than 10 feet away from you.   
**Agile:** Add \+5 to all dodges.

### Captain:  
**Covert Adept:** You can use your *Scout* ability twice per downtime instead of once.  
**Climbing Spikes:** You may climb up vertical surfaces with no penalty to movement.  
**Expert Survivalist:** Add an additional \+15 to all Tracking and Detection interactions. 

### Major:   
**Hearty:** Add \+9 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+7 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel:  
**Camouflage:** When making a Stealth interaction to hide, you may add an additional \+15 to the roll. Additionally, all creatures have a \-10 to Detection interactions while attempting to locate you.  
**Cloaking Devices:** 3 times per downtime, you can place a cloaking device on an object that could fit inside a 3 ft x 3 ft x 3 ft cube. The object will then be invisible to the naked eye, visible only with thermal imaging or similar technology. The object will remain invisible for up to one hour or until the cloaking devices are deactivated. It can be activated or deactivated remotely if you are within 2 miles of the object, however, the device must be attached manually.   
**Thermal Imaging Goggles:** Twice per downtime, for a minute each, you can activate thermal imaging goggles in your helmet that allow you to see and locate the thermal imaging of any creature within 60 feet, penetrating only through light underbrush and similar obstructions. These goggles allow you to ignore any penalties imposed by fighting in darkness. You can also read the current health and strength of the shields on up to 5 creatures.

### Colonel Major:  
**Tracer Rounds:** Each turn you may designate a single attack with a weapon that deals kinetic, plasma, or hardlight damage as being a tracer round. If this attack hits the target, then the outline of the target can be seen through walls and other objects from up to 80 feet away for the next hour.   
**Even the Odds:** If you make a weapon attack while exposed to the Invisible effect, the attack automatically deals an additional \+4 damage die. Additionally, if using a precision weapon, Add \+10 to the weapon’s Dodge DC when calling a shot. The attack still ends the Invisible effect as normal.  
**Improved Invisibility:** When using *Scout*, the Invisible effect now lasts for up to an hour, you gain \+10 to Stealth interactions while exposed to the Invisible effect, and you may use the Disarm, Switch and Grapple maneuvers without ending the Invisible effect. 

### Field Marshal:  
**Hearty:** Add \+13 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+11 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General:  
**Tripwire:** You have a tripwire that you can place as a Primary Operation, creating a line up to 20 feet long that requires a DC 85 detection check for someone who isn’t aware of its presence to find. Anyone who walks through  it must make a DC 80 dodge or be exposed to the fallen effect.  
**False Identity:** You have become skilled at the art of imitating others’ appearances. You can use an hour of downtime to transform your appearance into that of another creature of the same size category as you. At a glance, your appearance is indiscernible from that of which you are imitating, however upon closer inspection, some faults can be detected. When your disguise is inspected closely, you must make a Charisma check against the inspecting creature’s Detection check. On a success, they fall for your disguise, however, on a failure, the inspecting creature can see through the disguise.  
**Strike From the Shadows:** When attacking a creature exposed to the Surprised effect, your first attack grants an extra amount of damage die equal to the base weapon damage.

### General Major:  
**Hand to Hand Combatant:** While exposed to the Invisible effect, you may make an additional melee attack as part of your Primary Operation.  
**Ghillie Suit:** You can spend an hour constructing a ghillie suit according to an environment of your choice. When in the environment that it is designed for, you gain an additional \+20 to Stealth interactions and dodges. It takes a Primary Operation to put on or take off the suit. You may have no more than 1 ghillie suit in your possession at a time.  
**Bear Trap:** You have 2 bear traps that can be planted or buried anywhere you wish. Any creature of size large or smaller that steps on the trap, provided that there is no more than a foot of any material covering it, must succeed on a Dodge DC 95 or take 5d8 kinetic damage and be exposed to the Incapacitated effect. To end this effect, the creature must use a Primary Operation to attempt a DC 55 Lifting interaction to disarm the trap, or deal 20 damage to it.

### Commander:  
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+23 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+19 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as no Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander:  
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Off the Grid:** You cannot be detected by any technology that reveals your location (radar, thermal imaging goggles, etc).

### Hyper-Lethal Vector  
**Expert Tracker:** You have honed your abilities to the maximum allowed by the human species. You gain the following benefits permanently:  
\-You gain \+15 to all dodges.  
\-You gain \+15 to movement speed.  
\-You gain \+10 to all Tracking interactions.  
\-While invisible, you can take up to 40 damage before the effect ends.  
\-Add \+5 to all Dodge DCs for called shots.  
**Lone Wolf:** When attacking an enemy in direct line of sight, with no creatures within 15 feet of either you or your target, you gain the following benefits against only that creature. These do not apply when attacking or being attacked by another creature.  
\-You may make an additional attack with any weapon of large size or smaller  
\-Add \+10 to all Dodge DCs for called shots   
\-All attacks deal an extra \+6 (1d10) damage  
\-You gain a \+20 to all dodges

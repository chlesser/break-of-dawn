## Demolitionist
As command gives the order for the enemy base to be blown to smithereens, the Demolitionist happily presses the detonation button, watching the massive explosion in the distance. Seeing a survivor from the explosion, the spartan takes aim for a second, and launches a mortar shell from their shoulder, eliminating the last traces of their attack.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 55  
Shields: 60  
Health per rank up: 6 (1d10)  
Speed: 25 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: Grenade Launcher, Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Tech, Lifting  
Expertises: Firepower, Demolition  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 4 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 10 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Mortar.* Attached to your shoulder is a small mortar. Once per minute you can fire a shell as a Secondary Operation, with a range of 75 feet, dealing 5d8 Explosive damage, with Dodge DC 60 in a 10 foot radius. Creatures within the explosion radius that succeed on the dodge roll still take half damage (rounded down).

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+9 to your base health  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+5 to your base shields  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Bulky:** When enemies try to move you against your will, you gain a \+20 to dodges and contests to resist being moved.  
**Fortitude:** Twice per downtime when you take damage, as a Reactive Operation you may roll a d20 and reduce the damage taken by that amount.  
**Vigorous:** You receive a \+20 for any lifting check you make.

### Captain
**Demolitions Expert:** You can treat all explosive weapons as one size category smaller.  
**Grenadier:** When you use a grenade, its Dodge DC increases by 5, its range increases by 10 feet, and radius increases by 5 feet. Additionally, when you use a Primary Operation to throw a grenade, you may throw a second grenade without expending any Operations.  
**Twice the Pride:** When using a weapon with Detonation or grenade, you may designate a second main target granted that they are within 5 feet of the initial main target.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+17 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+11 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Repel:** As a reactionary Operation once every two turns, you may repel a single attack from a medium or smaller weapon that targets you or anything within 5 feet of you. The attacking creature then becomes the target of the attack but that attack’s Dodge DC is decreased by \-15.  
**Portable Energy Shield:** You gain an additional 80 shields, however these shields do not affect your total shield health or the amount of shields regenerated per turn of not taking damage. Instead, these additional shields generate a portable energy shield emanating around you. If the energy shield has between 1 and 30 shields, it grants Weak cover, if it has between 31 and 60 shields it grants Average cover, and if it has between 61 and 80 shields it grants Sturdy cover. If an attack would otherwise hit but is dodged as a result of the bonus provided from the portable energy shield’s cover, the shield takes the full damage of the attack. As a Secondary Operation, you can place this cover down anywhere within 5 feet. When deployed in this way it provides cover in a 15 foot line reaching 10 feet high. Any creatures behind this cover gain its benefits and can make weapon attacks through the shields with no penalty. As a Tertiary Operation, you may also pick the portable energy shield back up.  
**Anti-Gravity Armor:** Once per downtime, expending no Operations, you can activate a series of anti-gravity thrusters for up to ten minutes. These thrusters limit your speed to your base speed \-5 feet, however you can now walk on any surface, such as walls and ceilings. Additionally, difficult terrain does not limit your movement speed and you cannot take fall damage while this is active.

### Colonel Major
**Enhanced Area Effectiveness:** The Dodge DC for all enemies is increased by \+5 when using explosive weapons and grenades. Additionally, if a non-target fails the dodge by 15 or more, they take full damage instead of half damage.  
**Improved Explosives:** Whenever you hit a target with an explosive weapon, roll a d4. On a 4, roll twice the weapon’s base amount of damage dice.  
**Remote Charges:** Every downtime, you have five remote charges that you may use at will. Each remote charge does 18 (4d8) damage and can be set as Primary Operation. You may detonate these charges at any range within 5 miles of its location. These charges deal double damage to structures of any sort.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+26 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+19 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Armor Lock:** As a Primary Operation, you may enter into an armor lock where you stay for up to 2 turns or until you end it as a Secondary Operation. During this time you suffer a \-50 to all dodges and have your speed reduced to 0 feet, but you are immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When you exit armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage you took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge. This can be done up to twice per downtime.  
**Wrist Rocket:** As a modification to your armor, you may add a small armament of miniature rockets into your wrist plating. The attachment is capable of holding up to 5 rockets at a time, regaining all rockets during downtime. As a Primary Operation, you can fire as many of these rockets at a target as you wish, with each rocket dealing 4d4 explosive damage with a Dodge DC 80\. This attack can be split between multiple targets. For example, if you fire 5 rockets, you can fire 2 at one enemy and 3 on another, or any other combination of your choice.  
**Repelling Pulse:** As a Secondary Operation you can activate a pulse from your armor for up to 3 turns, which can be ended at any point on your turn as no Operation. At the start of your turn any creatures within 20 feet of you must make a dodge or be pushed away. the Dodge DC of this attack and the amount they are pushed away depends on the creature’s size. Small creatures must make a Dodge DC 80 or be pushed 15 feet. Medium creatures must make a Dodge DC 65 or be pushed 10 feet. Large Creatures must make a Dodge DC 50 or be pushed 5 feet. 

### General Major
**Multi-Mortar:** You gain two more different methods of using your mortar. They are as follows:  
\-Carpet Bombs: You pepper a 25 by 25 foot cube with smaller explosive pellets with a range of 40 feet and Dodge DC of 65\. All creatures within this area take 20 (3d12) explosive damage. Any creatures that succeed take half damage (rounded down).  
\-Homing Missile: You launch a seeking mortar shell that targets an individual target. The shell has a range of 100 feet, has a Dodge DC 85, and deals 33 (6d10) explosive damage. On a successful dodge, the target takes half damage (rounded down)..  
**Shockwaves:** Your explosives now release shockwaves upon ignition. Whenever a creature fails their dodge against an attack made by you with an explosive weapon by 15 or more, they are exposed to the fallen effect. Additionally, you deal double damage to all structures and buildings with explosive weapons.  
**Vehicle Buster:** When attacking a vehicle with explosive weapons, add \+10 to your weapon’s Dodge DC. If the vehicle fails the dodge by 20 or more, you may roll twice the weapon’s base amount of damage dice.

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+40 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+33 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Berserker:** When you fall below half base health, you take half damage (rounded down) from all attacks. Additionally, all melee attacks deal twice the attack’s base amount of damage dice.

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Landmine:** You may spend an hour to create up to two landmines a foot across and a foot wide. These mines can be detonated from up to 5 miles away, or when triggered by at least five pounds of pressure. This mine can be attached to any surface or buried underground, taking five extra pounds of pressure to detonate for each foot underground it is. Any creature who steps on the landmine instantly takes 28 (5d10) explosive damage with no dodge, and all creatures within 20 feet must make a Dodge DC 80 or take full damage and be exposed to the Fallen effect, but only takes half damage (rounded down) on a success. The landmine can also be thrown up to 20 feet if so chosen. It takes a Primary Operation to detonate a landmine.  
**Explosive Confidence:** As a Primary Operation once per downtime your explosive antics inspire your companions. Any allies, including you, within 30 feet of you may use a grenade as a Secondary Operation, and gain any abilities related to explosive damage and weapons that you have. This aura lasts for a minute.

## Assassin
The grass rustles as a Spartan armed only with a knife soundlessly sneaks up on his prey; an elite shipmaster. As he comes up behind him, the elite only has time for a single muffled scream before his throat is slit by the Assassin’s knife. With his objective accomplished, he slinks back into the grass, waiting for his next victim. He is an assassin, a precision oriented class that stalks its prey and eliminates them with an array of specialized melee weaponry in addition to standard firearms.

**Statistics:**
Health: 30  
Shields: 35  
Health per rank up: 4 (1d6)  
Speed: 45 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: A Shotgun and a Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Tracking, Charisma  
Expertises: Stealth, Agility  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 3 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 5 grenade slots

### Starting Abilities
**Assassinate**. When you make a melee attack against a creature exposed to the Surprised effect, you may add an additional \+22 (4d10) kinetic damage.  
**Combat Knife**. You have a knife you may use instead of the normal melee ability. The knife can be thrown up to 20 feet with a dodge of 50 dealing the same amount of kinetic damage as a melee attack but does not count as a melee attack. 

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+4 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+3 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Fast:** Gain an extra \+5 movement speed.  
**Suppressor:** Ranged weapon attacks you make cannot be heard by creatures further than 10 feet away from you.   
**Agile:** Add \+5 to all dodges.

### Captain
**Improved Combat Knife:** You have improved your skills with knives. The Dodge DC of your knife increases to 65 when held or thrown. Additionally, the *Assassinate* ability applies to thrown knives.  
**Hide in the Shadows:** When in complete darkness, you may roll a Stealth interaction. No matter the result, you are exposed to the Invisible effect. However, a creature that makes a Detection interaction that has a higher total than your stealth roll is able to see you. This ability does not protect you from thermal imaging.    
**Nimble:** You do not trigger chance attacks when moving. Additionally, you gain a \+10 to all dodges.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+7 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+5 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Camouflage:** When making a Stealth interaction to hide, you may add an additional \+15 to the roll. Additionally, all creatures have a \-10 to Detection interactions while attempting to locate you.  
**Cloaking Devices:** 3 times per downtime, you can place a cloaking device on an object that could fit inside a 3 ft x 3 ft x 3 ft cube. The object will then be invisible to the naked eye, visible only with thermal imaging or similar technology. The object will remain invisible for up to one hour or until the cloaking devices are deactivated. It can be activated or deactivated remotely if you are within 2 miles of the object, however, the device must be attached manually.   
**Thermal Imaging Goggles:** Twice per downtime, for a minute each, you can activate thermal imaging goggles in your helmet that allow you to see and locate the thermal imaging of any creature within 60 feet, penetrating only through light underbrush and similar obstructions. These goggles allow you to ignore any penalties imposed by fighting in darkness. You can also read the current health and strength of the shields on up to 5 creatures.

### Colonel Major
**Improved Melee**: The Dodge DC of your melee attacks increases by 15\.  
**Twist of Fate:** Twice per downtime when you fail a dodge you can choose to succeed instead.  
**Weapons Experts:** You may select up to two weapons of small size which you have grown akin to using. You may now use the *Assassinate* ability with their attacks.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+15 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+12 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Tripwire:** You have a tripwire that you can place as a Primary Operation, creating a line up to 20 feet long that requires a DC 85 detection check for someone who isn’t aware of its presence to find. Anyone who walks through  it must make a DC 80 dodge or be exposed to the fallen effect.  
**False Identity:** You have become skilled at the art of imitating others’ appearances. You can use an hour of downtime to transform your appearance into that of another creature of the same size category as you. At a glance, your appearance is indiscernible from that of which you are imitating, however upon closer inspection, some faults can be detected. When your disguise is inspected closely, you must make a Charisma check against the inspecting creature’s Detection check. On a success, they fall for your disguise, however, on a fail, the inspecting creature can see through the disguise.  
**Strike From the Shadows:**  When attacking a creature exposed to the Surprised effect, your first attack grants an extra amount of damage die equal to the base weapon damage.

### General Major
**Assassination Expert**: When using your *Assassinate* ability, add an additional \+22 (4d10) kinetic damage to the base damage, additionally the Dodge DC for punch/stab attacks made against surprised enemies increases by \+10.  
**Active Camo:** Twice per downtime, as a Tertiary Operation you may activate an invisibility module within your suit. When activated, you and any equipment you were carrying at the time of its activation are exposed to the Invisible effect. This lasts for up to a minute or until you end it as a Secondary Operation or make a weapon attack.   
**Martial Arts Expert:** When you make a stab/punch attack, you can make a second attack with a small weapon or another stab/punch attack with \-10 subtracted from the Dodge DC. 

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+24 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+19 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability of Commander rank or lower from any class’s list.  
**Off the Grid:** You cannot be detected by any technology that reveals your location (radar, thermal imaging goggles, etc).

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Target Acquired:** During combat as a Secondary Operation you may choose an enemy within 30 feet to mark as a target. When attacking a marked target you gain an additional Secondary and Tertiary Operation, provided that all of your attacks and maneuvers are made against that target. Your Dodge DC increases by \+10 for all attacks and maneuvers against that target. If your target exits your melee/stab range, you may use a Reactive Operation to make a melee/stab attack dealing half damage (rounded down). After a target is killed, you can transfer these effects to another creature within 30 feet as a Tertiary Operation.   
**Athlete:** Your body has reached the peak agility of what a Spartan can accomplish and gain the following abilities:  
\-You gain \+15 movement speed  
\-You gain \+15 to all dodges  
\-When you kill an enemy, you can immediately move up to 15 feet in any direction, not    
counting against your movement speed.   
\-The Dodge DC of your punch/stab increases by 10\.

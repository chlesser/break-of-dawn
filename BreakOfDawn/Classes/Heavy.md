## Heavy 
A massive warrior comes barreling through his foes, with a chaingun in hand. His enemies cower before him as he weathers all of their attacks, leaving a trail of dead Covenant in his wake. This is the heavy, a class known to be able to withstand enormous amounts of damage while annihilating all enemies in their path with a massive detached turret.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 60  
Shields: 75  
Health per rank up: 7 (1d12)  
Speed: 20 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: Chaingun  
Superiorities: Firepower, Charisma  
Expertises: Fortitude, Lifting  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 5 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 8 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Charge:* Twice per downtime, as a Secondary Operation and Tertiary Operation you can choose to charge 1 target. When you charge you gain an additional 10 feet of movement speed. You then must spend at least 10 feet of movement moving toward an enemy, but do not provoke any chance attacks. If you end your movement within 5 feet of a target, the target must then make a Dodge DC 60 or take 28 (8d6) kinetic damage and be exposed to the Fallen effect. 

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+10 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+7 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Bulky:** When enemies try to move you against your will, you gain a \+20 to dodges and contests to resist being moved.  
**Fortitude:** Twice per downtime, as a Reactive Operation, when you take damage, you may roll a d20 and reduce the damage taken by that amount.   
**Vigorous:** You may add \+20 to any lifting interactions you make.

### Captain
**Large Weapons Expert:** When using a weapon of size large or larger, add an extra \+1 damage die.  
**Interpose:** As a reactionary Operation, you may interpose yourself between an ally and any single attack targeting that ally provided you are within 5 feet of your ally, with you taking the full damage of the attack and the ally taking no damage as a result. If the ally was the target for a radius-based attack, you become the target instead.  
**Strong:** You ignore movement penalties made by carrying turrets and may use all combat maneuvers while carrying a turret.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+20 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+12 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Repel:** As a reactionary Operation once every two turns, you may repel a single attack from a medium or smaller weapon that targets you or anything within 5 feet of you. The attacking creature then becomes the target of the attack but that attack’s Dodge DC is decreased by \-15.  
**Portable Energy Shield:** You gain an additional 80 shields, however these shields do not affect your total shield health or the amount of shields regenerated per turn of not taking damage. Instead, these additional shields generate a portable energy shield emanating around you. If the energy shield has between 1 and 30 shields, it grants Weak cover, if it has between 31 and 60 shields it grants Average cover, and if it has between 61 and 80 shields it grants Sturdy cover. If an attack would otherwise hit but is dodged as a result of the bonus provided from the portable energy shield’s cover, the shield takes the full damage of the attack. As a Secondary Operation, you can place this cover down anywhere within 5 feet. When deployed in this way it provides cover in a 15 foot line reaching 10 feet high. Any creatures behind this cover gain its benefits and can make weapon attacks through the shields with no penalty. As a Tertiary Operation, you may also pick the portable energy shield back up.  
**Anti-Gravity Armor:** Once per day, expending no Operations, you can activate a series of anti-gravity thrusters for up to ten minutes. These thrusters limit your speed to your base speed minus 5, however you can now walk on any surface, such as walls and ceilings. Additionally, difficult terrain does not limit your movement speed and you cannot take fall damage while this is active.

### Colonel Major
**Strength of the Ram:** Your *Charge* damage increases by \+11 (3d6) kinetic damage, and the Dodge DC increases by 10\. Additionally, you gain another use of *Charge* per downtime.  
**Thundering Blow:** The force of your blow is now enough to knock your enemies off of their feet. If you make a successful melee attack, the target must make a DC 90 Fortitude interaction if they are a small creature, a DC 75 Fortitude interaction if they are a medium sized creature, and a DC 50 Fortitude interaction if they are a large creature. On a failure the target is pushed 10 feet away from you and exposed to the Fallen effect. Additionally, all of your melee attacks do an additional \+4d6 damage.  
**Last Stand**: When no other conscious allies are within 25 feet of you, you may make an additional weapon attack, and gain an additional Secondary and Tertiary Operation.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+40 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+24 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Armor Lock:**  As a Primary Operation, you may enter into an armor lock where you stay for up to 2 turns or until you end it as a Secondary Operation. During this time you suffer a \-50 to all dodges and have your speed reduced to 0 feet, but you are immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When you exit armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage you took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge. This can be done up to twice per downtime.  
**Wrist Rocket:** As a modification to your armor, you may add a small armament of miniature rockets into your wrist plating. The attachment is capable of holding up to 5 rockets at a time, regaining all rockets during downtime. As a Primary Operation, you can fire as many of these rockets at a target as you wish, with each rocket dealing 4d4 explosive damage with a Dodge DC 80\. This attack can be split between multiple targets. For example, if you fire 5 rockets, you can fire 2 at one enemy and 3 on another, or any other combination of your choice.  
**Repelling Pulse:** As a Secondary Operation you can activate a pulse from your armor for up to 3 turns, which can be ended at any point on your turn as no Operation. At the start of your turn any creatures within 20 feet of you must make a dodge or be pushed away. the Dodge DC of this attack and the amount they are pushed away depends on the creature’s size. Small creatures must make a Dodge DC 80 or be pushed 15 feet. Medium creatures must make a Dodge DC 65 or be pushed 10 feet. Large Creatures must make a Dodge DC 50 or be pushed 5 feet.                                                                                                                                       
### General Major
**Juggernaut:** Your physical fortitude has allowed you to stretch the human body to its limit. Whenever you are knocked unconscious, you may instead choose to drop to 1 health instead. You may do this once per downtime for free, however for each time after, you must subtract \-5 from each of your rolls, and enemies get an additional \+5 to dodging your attacks. This penalty is cumulative, however if you reach a penalty of \-30, you are unable to regain consciousness via this ability again until the penalty is lessened or ends. After each downtime, you may take away up to 20 of this penalty.  
**Steel Will:** As a Secondary Operation you may forfeit your movement until the end of your next turn to take half damage (rounded down) from all attacks until the end of your next turn. This can be done 2 times per downtime.  
**Battering Ram:** Add an additional \+4d6 damage die to your *Charge* ability and gain 2 more uses of this ability per downtime. In addition, you have become an expert at dealing with multiple targets. You can now make your *Charge* attack against a second target within 5 feet of your initial target.

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+60 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+38 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Berserker:** While below half of your maximum health, you take half damage (rounded down) from all attacks. Additionally, all melee attacks deal double damage while in this state.	

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Seismic Charge:** Twice per downtime, as a Primary Operation, you can release a seismic charge originating from your character. You can force every hostile creature within 20 feet of you to make a DC 90 dodge or be exposed to the Fallen effect and take 8d10 kinetic damage, or half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.   
**Unwavering Defender:** Once per downtime, as a Secondary Operation, you can enter a state of heightened awareness that lasts for 5 minutes. During this time, you and all allies within 10 feet of you regain up to 5 health back at the start of each of their turns, and may increase the damage of all their weapon attacks by one die.

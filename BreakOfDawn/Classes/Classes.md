## Fireteam  
The gameplay of Break of Dawn is centered around a unit of player-controlled Spartans called a Fireteam. Individual members of the Fireteam will have unique skills and abilities that come from their homeworld, background and class. A spartan’s class represents the role they will play in their Fireteam. The basic classes are as follows:

| Class        | Superiorities      | Expertises       | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------ | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Assassin]] | Tracking, Charisma | Stealth, Agility | A stealth based fighter that uses close-quarters combat to eliminate enemies from the shadows. |
| [[Commando]] |                    |                  |                                                                                                |

1. **[[Assassin]]**
   A stealth based fighter that uses close-quarters combat to eliminate enemies from the shadows.  
2. **Commando**   
   A versatile combatant who can adapt their strategies to nearly any environment or situation, making them a vital asset to any mission.  
3. **Demolitionist:**  
   A master of munitions, this class excels with explosives of all sorts from blanketing battlefields in mortar fire to laying volatile traps for their enemies.   
4. **Engineer:**  
   A battlefield mechanic with a specialty in modifying and repairing weaponry and vehicles as the situation requires.  
5. **Heavy:**  
   A powerful warrior capable of weathering assaults that would render other Spartans incapacitated.  
6. **Leader:**   
   A fearless leader, who issues orders to the rest of their Fireteam to coordinate strategies in order to gain the upper hand in even the thickest of fights.   
7. **Sniper:**  
   A light but deadly attacker that deals massive damage from the fringes of the battlefield.  
8. **Tech:**   
   A support class that uses their technical skills to empower their allies and rescue downed comrades.   
9. **Tracker:**  
   A specialist that excels in tracking down and eliminating targets, as well as identifying potential threats before they become problems.

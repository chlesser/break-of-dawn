## Sniper
A lone man sits in a sniper perch, waiting for his quarry to come into his sights. Camouflaged against the backdrop of his surroundings, not a single soul can see him. As a Covenant Zealot enters his sniper’s scope, he aims and pulls the trigger. This is the sniper, a long-range warrior capable of dealing immense damage without having to get near the fight.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 15  
Shields: 25  
Health per rank up: 3 (1d4)  
Speed: 35 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: Sniper Rifle, Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Agility, Tech  
Expertises: Detection, Stealth  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 3 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 5 grenade slots

### Starting Ability:
*Precision Aim.* 5 times per downtime, when you make a ranged weapon attack, you may choose to use this ability to instantly kill an enemy that has up to 75 total health & shields remaining if the attack hits them. You must choose to do so before you make the attack.

### 2nd Lieutenant:  
**Hearty:** Add \+3 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+2 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant:  
**Fast:** Gain an extra \+5 feet of movement speed.  
**Suppressor:** Ranged weapon attacks you make cannot be heard by creatures further than 10 feet away from you.  
**Agile:** Add \+5 to all dodges.

### Captain:  
**Sniper Expert:** When using a weapon with a called shot bonus of \+15, add an additional \+2 damage die to the attack.  
**Hip Fire:** When within 10 feet of an enemy, the Dodge DC of your weapon attacks increases by \+10.  
**Take Aim**: You forfeit your entire turn to take extra time to line up an attack. When you make an attack on your next turn, the Dodge DC increases by 40 and deals an additional \+4 damage die.

### Major:  
**Hearty:** Add \+5 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+4 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel:  
**Camouflage:** When making a Stealth interaction to hide, you may add an additional \+15 to the roll. Additionally, all creatures have a \-10 to Detection interactions while attempting to locate you.  
**Cloaking Devices:** 3 times per downtime, you can place a cloaking device on an object that could fit inside a 3 ft x 3 ft x 3 ft cube. The object will then be invisible to the naked eye, visible only with thermal imaging or similar technology. The object will remain invisible for up to one hour or until the cloaking devices are deactivated. It can be activated or deactivated remotely if you are within 2 miles of the object, however, the device must be attached manually.   
**Thermal Imaging Goggles:** Twice per downtime, for a minute each, you can activate thermal imaging goggles in your helmet that allow you to see and locate the thermal imaging of any creature within 60 feet, penetrating only through light underbrush and similar obstructions. These goggles allow you to ignore any penalties imposed by fighting in darkness. You can also read the current health and strength of the shields on up to 5 creatures.

### Colonel Major:  
**Ambush:** If you make an attack against a creature that is exposed to the Surprised effect, you may treat the attack as a called shot of your choice if the creature fails the dodge by any amount. Additionally, any creature you attack in this manner has an additional \-15 penalty to their dodge.  
**Flawless Aim:** You can instantly kill an enemy with up to 150 total base health and shields remaining as part of your Precision Aim ability.  
**Shotcaller:** When making a called shot, your weapon’s called shot bonus increases by \+5, However the dodge penalty for the called shot cannot be reduced beyond \-0. In addition, your called shots become more effective in the following ways:  
Leg Shots: The target’s speed is now decreased by \-20 feet  
Arm Shots: The target will now drop anything in their hands on a roll of 1 or 2\.  
Headshots: Add an additional \+3 damage die.

### Field Marshal:  
**Hearty:** Add \+11 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+9 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General:  
**Tripwire:** You have a tripwire that you can place as a Primary Operation, creating a line up to 20 feet long that requires a DC 85 detection check for someone who isn’t aware of its presence to find. Anyone who walks through  it must make a DC 80 dodge or be exposed to the fallen effect.  
**False Identity:** You have become skilled at the art of imitating others’ appearances. You can use an hour of downtime to transform your appearance into that of another creature of the same size category as you. At a glance, your appearance is indiscernible from that of which you are imitating, however upon closer inspection, some faults can be detected. When your disguise is inspected closely, you must make a Charisma check against the inspecting creature’s Detection check. On a success, they fall for your disguise, however, on a fail, the inspecting creature can see through the disguise.  
**Strike From the Shadows:**  When attacking a creature exposed to the Surprised effect, your first attack grants an extra amount of damage die equal to the base amount of weapon damage die.

### General Major:  
**Armor Piercing Rounds:** 8 times per downtime, you may choose to use specialized armor piercing rounds. You must specify that you are using one of these rounds before you make your attack. When you choose to use these rounds, half of the damage dealt is dealt to the shields of a creature, while the other half is dealt to the base health. If this damage breaks the creatures’ shields, any extra is applied to the base health. If the creature has no shields, then it deals an extra 3 damage die to the specified target.  
**Penetrating Shot:** When attacking a creature, if the damage of your attack exceeds the health of that creature, the remaining damage can be transferred to another creature within a 15 foot line behind the defeated creature. The second creature must then make a dodge with 15 subtracted from the weapons Dodge DC. If the creature fails, then they take half of the remaining damage. It is possible for this damage to pierce through multiple enemies.  
**Small Weapons Expert:** After using your Primary Operation, you can switch to a single small weapon expending a Secondary and Tertiary Operation. You may then make up to 3 attacks with that weapon, however it cannot exceed the maximum number of times that weapon can be fired in a turn as described in its statistics. You then have the option to swap your weapon again using no Operation.

### Commander:  
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+18 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+15 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as no Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander:  
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Off the Grid:** You cannot be detected by any technology that reveals your location (radar, thermal imaging goggles, etc).

### Hyper-Lethal Vector:  
**Sonar:** The built-in sonar module in your helmet makes you aware of the location of all creatures without an active camo module within 30 feet of you. When attempting to shoot through a barrier targeting creature that you can locate with your sonar, you may treat that barrier as 3 levels lower than its stated level.   
**Ricochet:** You have gained the ability to ricochet your shots off of applicable surfaces. After each ricochet, roll a d100. If you get below the number specified in the table below, the shot cannot ricochet off of any more surfaces. The range of the weapon is unaffected by the number of times the bullet ricochets on an object. The different surfaces that the shot can be ricocheted off of and their penalties are as follows:

*Flimsy*: cannot ricochet  
*Weak*: \-25 to weapon’s dodge, 60 or above to continue ricochet  
*Average*: \-20 to weapon’s dodge, 50 or above to continue ricochet  
*Sturdy*: \-15 to weapon’s dodge, 40 or above to continue ricochet  
*Solid*: \-10 to weapon’s dodge, 30 or above to continue ricochet  
*Strong*: \-5 to weapon’s dodge, 20 or above to continue ricochet  
*Unassailable*: weapon’s dodge unaffected, 10 or above to continue ricochet

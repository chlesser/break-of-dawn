## Engineer
While the battle rages on, a Spartan on the backlines works on a mechanical appliance that resembles a turret. After only a few minutes, the turret is online, and opens fire on the Covenant forces. She eliminates the last few grunts with her dual SMGs, claiming the battle as a UNSC victory. The engineer is a highly-intelligent support unit who relies on an automated combat turret in tight situations.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 45  
Shields: 55  
Health per rank up: 6 (1d10)  
Speed: 30 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: Two SMGs  
Superiorities: Tech, Handling  
Expertises: Crafting, Firepower  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 3 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 6 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Turret.* As a Primary Operation you can deploy a chaingun turret anywhere within 5 feet that operates on its own, taking its turn directly after yours. This turret has 75 health with a dodge penalty of \-30. As a Primary Operation you may quickly repair your turret by making a Crafting interaction with a DC equal to the total amount of health lost by the turret, restoring half of the turret’s lost health on a success (rounded down), provided you are within 5 feet of your turret. When it is not in use, it regains 5 health per minute. If the turret reaches 0 health, you must spend an uninterrupted hour to repair the turret, restoring it to maximum health, or crafting a new one if it was lost. You may have no more than 1 turret at a time. While within 5 feet of the turret, you may deactivate and store the turret as a Secondary Operation, placing it on the Spartan’s back where it takes up no inventory space.

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+8 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+5 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Agile:** Add \+5 to all dodges.  
**Medic:** During a downtime, you can create up to 3 medpacs.  
**Disable:** As a Secondary Operation you can disable any simple electronic device that you could normally influence with the Tech interaction within 20 feet of you for up to 1 minute. 

### Captain
**Master Craftsman:** You may spend 10 minutes to create a machine that adds \+20 to a single type of interaction while in your possession. You may only have one of these machines active at one time.   
**Ammo Dispensary:** You may spend 10 uninterrupted minutes to craft up to 5 Ammo Die for any weapon. These do not all have to be for the same weapon, but they cannot exceed the maximum ammo die allowed for by the weapon.  
**Combat Medicine**: 3 times per downtime, you may spend a Secondary Operation to grant a creature within 5 feet of you up to \+20 health. 

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+13 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+8 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Anti-Gravity Armor:** Once per day you can activate a series of anti-gravity thrusters for up to 10 minutes. These thrusters limit your speed to your base speed minus 5, however you can now walk on any surface, such as walls and ceilings. Additionally, difficult terrain does not limit your movement speed and you cannot take fall damage while this is active.  
**Thermal Imaging Goggles:** Twice per downtime, for a minute each, you can activate thermal imaging goggles in your helmet that allow you to see and locate the thermal imaging of any creature within 60 feet, penetrating only through light underbrush and similar obstructions. These goggles allow you to ignore any penalties imposed by fighting in darkness. You can also read the current health and strength of the shields on up to 5 creatures.  
**Doctor:** When using a medpac, the health regained is now equal to the amount rolled on a Medicinal interaction without halving it, adding all bonuses.

### Colonel Major
**Weapon Creation:** You can spend an uninterrupted hour to create up to 3 gun slots worth of UNSC weaponry. These weapons can be changed out any time you spend an hour doing so. **Improved Turret:** When you deploy your turret, you may now choose whether it takes the form of a chaingun, gauss, or rocket turret. The chosen type can be changed when you next deploy your turret. In addition, your turret now has 100 base health.   
**Shoulder Turret:** As a Secondary Operation, you may activate a shoulder-mounted turret. As a Tertiary Operation, you may have the turret make up to 2 attacks. The turret has Dodge DC 60, and deals 9 (2d8)  kinetic damage on a hit. This turret lasts for up to a minute and can be ended early as a Tertiary Operation. This can be done up to twice per downtime.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+22 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+18 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Tripwire:** You have a tripwire that you can place as a Primary Operation, creating a line up to 20 feet long that requires a DC 85 detection check for someone who isn’t aware of its presence to find. Anyone who walks through it must make a DC 80 dodge or be exposed to the fallen effect.  
**Immunity to Effects:** As a Tertiary Operation you may cleanse an ally within 30 feet of 1 effect other than wounded.  
**Tactical Advantage:** As a Reactive Operation, when you or an ally within 30 feet of you makes a dodge you may add a \+20 to the dodge. You must do this before you know the result of the dodge.

### General Major
**Vehicle Rewire:** Twice per downtime, you can use Primary Operation to rewire a vehicle's hardware to rig it to explode at a time of your choosing. The vehicle must be within 5 feet, but after rewiring the vehicle, you may choose to detonate it anytime between 1-5 rounds after rewiring it. Until the designated time of explosion, the vehicle operates as normal.   
**Attachment Crafting:** Your expertise in engineering lets you create up to 5 gun attachment slots. To do so you must spend an hour crafting per slot. Slots are determined as follows:

- Tier I: 1 slot  
- Tier II: 2 slots  
- Tier III: 3 slots  
- Tier IV: 4 slots  
- Tier V: 5 slots

If you wish to change the attachments you have crafted, you must spend the necessary amount of time to create a new attachment and dismantle the previous attachments.  
**Autosentry:** As a Primary Operation you can create an autosentry using this ability. This autosentry stays in place for up to four rounds unless it is destroyed or you take a Tertiary Operation to dismiss it. The autosentry takes its turn directly after your own. You may deploy up to 3 autosentries per downtime.

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+31 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+26 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled..  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Retaliatory Surge:** As a Reactive Operation, after all of your shield health has been depleted, you release a surge of energy centered around yourself. Any target of your choice within 20 feet must succeed on a DC 85 dodge or take 28 (5d10) shock damage (DC 65 Fortitude).

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Electrocution Pods:** You have 5 electrocution pods that can be used once each per downtime. They can be placed or thrown as no Operation and detonated as a Secondary Operation. Each pod is only a few inches wide and when detonated emits an electric charge targeting up to 5 creatures of the player’s choice within 10 feet of the pod. Each targeted creature must make a DC 80 dodge or take 18 (4d8) damage shock damage (DC 60 Fortitude) and be stunned until the end of your next turn. You cannot detonate more than 1 pod per turn.   
**Damage Absorption Suit Upgrade:** You have a module within your armor that allows you to absorb up to 200 points of damage. When you make a dodge (against an attack dealing damage) that exceeds the Dodge DC by 15 or more, you may absorb the damage that would have been dealt to you. The extra damage acts as a pool that you can draw on, and you may add a chosen amount of damage from this pool to the damage dealt by an attack you make, with the extra damage becoming the same damage type as is dealt by the attack.

## Commando
A single Spartan charges a line of elites, wielding an assault rifle with deadly efficiency. The elites start to retreat as the Spartan methodically carves a hole into their ranks. As his enemies drop around him, he proceeds to claim the Forerunner artifact he had worked so hard to find. An all-around powerhouse, the commando is the perfect example of the archetypal Spartan; adaptable, adroit, and deadly.

**Statistics**:  
Health: 40  
Shields: 40  
Health per rank up: 5 (1d8)  
Speed: 35 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: An Assault Rifle and a Magnum Pistol  
Superiorities: Fortitude, Handling  
Expertises: Firepower, Agility  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 3 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 6 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Power Surge*: Twice per downtime, on your turn you can choose to take an additional Primary Operation. 

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+7 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+4 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Well-Rounded:** You may add \+2 damage to all weapon attacks.  
**Fast:** Gain an extra \+5 movement speed.  
**Medic:** During a downtime, you can create up to 3 medpacs.

### Captain
**Revitalize:** Twice per downtime, you may use a Secondary Operation to regain \+9 (2d8) health.   
**Threat Sensor:** As a Secondary Operation, you may fire a recon dart up to 40 feet. The heat signatures of any creature within 10 feet of the dart’s location are exposed to any creature within 40 feet of the dart’s location.  
**Improved Power Surge:** When using *Power Surge*, you gain your Secondary and Tertiary Operation as well as your Primary Operation.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+12 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+8 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Flank:** When making an attack against an enemy that did not make an attack against you on their previous turn, they have a \-5 to dodges and you deal an additional 1d4 damage of that weapon’s damage type per attack.  
**Flash Mines:** You can deploy up to three small flash mines per downtime, and each can be placed as a Secondary Operation. They can be stuck onto surfaces, buried, or thrown up to 20 feet, and can be detonated as a Tertiary Operation. Any creature within 5 feet of the mine must succeed on a DC 50 dodge or be exposed to the Deafened and Blinded effects until the end of your next turn.   
**Pilot:** You become much more efficient at using vehicles. Therefore, when in the driver seat of a vehicle, moving the vehicle becomes no Operation, using the Ram ability becomes a Tertiary Operation, and using the vehicle’s weapons becomes a Secondary Operation.

### Colonel Major
**Flamethrower:** Every creature in a 15 foot cone in front of you must make a DC 55 dodge or be caught in the flamethrower. If the creature succeeds they take no damage, but if they fail they take 14 (3d8) thermal damage. Additionally, any flammable object within its range catches on fire.  
**Jetpack:** You gain a fly speed equal to your movement speed.    
**Push Beyond Limits:** You may now use *Power Surge* 3 times per downtime.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+21 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+16 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Armor Lock:** As a Primary Operation, you may enter into an armor lock where you stay for up to 2 turns or until you end it as a Secondary Operation. During this time you suffer a \-50 to all dodges and have your speed reduced to 0 feet, but you are immune to all damage and negative effects except that which the GM considers unreasonable (such as a nuclear explosion). When you exit armor lock any hostile creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dodge DC 80 or take however much damage you took while armor locked as shock damage (Fortitude DC 60), taking half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge. This can be done up to twice per downtime.  
**Wrist Rocket:** As a modification to your armor, you may add a small armament of miniature rockets into your wrist plating. The attachment is capable of holding up to 5 rockets at a time, regaining all rockets during downtime. As a Primary Operation, you can fire as many of these rockets at a target as you wish, with each rocket dealing 4d4 explosive damage with a Dodge DC 80\. This attack can be split between multiple targets. For example, if you fire 5 rockets, you can fire 2 at one enemy and 3 on another, or any other combination of your choice.  
**Ace:** Pick 2 abilities from which you already have expertise/superiority, you gain a \+25 to check with these abilities.

### General Major
**Remote Charges:** Every downtime, you have five remote charges that you may use at will. Each remote charge does 18 (4d8) explosive damage and can be set as a Primary Operation. You may detonate these charges at any range within 5 miles of its location. These charges deal double damage to structures of any sort.  
**Piloting Expertise:** When operating a vehicle, you may add an additional \+10 feet to its movement speed, add 2 more damage die to its Ram damage, and make an additional attack with the vehicle's weapons.  
**Take Command:** Once per downtime you may share the effects of your *Power Surge* with two other allies of your choice within 10 feet of you.

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+33 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+25 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as a Tertiary Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Master of the Many:** You gain a \+15 to all ability checks you do not have expertise or superiority in.

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Chain Attack:** You may choose up to 5 enemies that are no more than 20 feet away from each other. When you make an attack against one of these enemies, the damage and effects of that attack apply to all chosen enemies. You may do this up to four times per downtime.  
**Combat Efficiency:** When you kill an enemy on your turn, you may make an additional number of attacks with the weapon you used to kill them equal to half its typical amount (rounded down).

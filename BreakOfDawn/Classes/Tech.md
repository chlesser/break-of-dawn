## Tech
A hacker sits at her computer attempting to upload a virus into the enemies’ security system. Her fingers are flying as everything from cameras to turrets start to come offline. She shuts off her computer as the entire security system comes crashing down around her. This is the Tech, a technologically savvy supporter who has the ability to hack both her allies’, or her enemies’ shields.

**Statistics:**  
Health: 35  
Shields: 40  
Health per rank up: 5 (1d8)  
Speed: 35 feet  
Size: Medium  
Recommended Starting Weaponry: 2 Magnum Pistols  
Superiorities: History, Medicinal  
Expertises: Handling, Tech  
Gun Carrying Capacity: You have 2 gun slots  
Grenade Capacity: You have 4 grenade slots

### Starting Ability
*Shield Recharge.* 5 times per downtime, you can use your Secondary Operation to recharge the shields of an ally within 30 ft of you and restore their shields by half (rounded down). You can restore your own shields in this way too.

### 2nd Lieutenant
**Hearty:** Add \+7 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+5 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt**: Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+5 Interaction Points.

### 1st Lieutenant
**Agile:** Add \+5 to all dodges.  
**Medic:** During a downtime, you can create up to 3 medpacs.  
**Disable:** As a Secondary Operation you can disable any simple electronic device that you could normally influence with the Tech interaction within 20 feet of you for up to 1 minute. 

### Captain
**Tech Expert:** Add an additional \+15 to all Tech and Handling interactions.  
**Listening Device:** You are given a total of 5 listening devices, capable of picking up sound audibly in a 30 foot radius. They are small, only a few inches across and long, and can be attached to any solid surface. While within one mile of a listening device, you are aware of all sounds made in the 30 foot radius.  If one is lost or destroyed, you may spend the next downtime creating up to 5 new listening devices, with a maximum of 5 listening devices carried  
**Medical Foam:** As a Secondary Operation, you may target one dying creature within 5 feet. Any creature you target in this way is stabilized and no longer suffers repeat damage from the attack that downed them. This effect lasts for up to an hour, or until damage equal to half (rounded down) of that creature’s base health is dealt to them.

### Major
**Hearty:** Add \+11 to your base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+8 to your base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+1 grenade slot.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+1 gun slot.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+10 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant Colonel
**Anti-Gravity Armor:** Once per day you can activate a series of anti-gravity thrusters for up to ten minutes. These thrusters limit your speed to your base speed minus 5, however you can now walk on any surface, such as walls and ceilings. Additionally, difficult terrain does not limit your movement speed and you cannot take fall damage while this is active.  
**Thermal Imaging Goggles:** Twice per downtime, for a minute each, you can activate thermal imaging goggles in your helmet that allow you to see and locate the thermal imaging of any creature within 60 feet, penetrating only through light underbrush and similar obstructions. These goggles allow you to ignore any penalties imposed by fighting in darkness. You can also read the current health and strength of the shields on up to 5 creatures.  
**Doctor:** When using a medpac, the health regained is now equal to the amount rolled on a medicinal check, adding all bonuses.

### Colonel Major
**EMP:** 3 times per downtime, as Primary Operation, you may use an EMP grenade. The grenade can be thrown up to 30 feet, and has a radius of 10 feet. Any creatures or vehicles within that range must make a DC 85 dodge or be exposed to the Stunned effect. Any vehicle caught in the radius must complete a DC 60 dodge or be rendered immobile and unable to fire any weapons other than turrets. The stunned effect lasts for 3 rounds, or until the creature completes the dodge, which they may remake as a Primary Operation at the end of each of their turns, with the Dodge DC decreasing by ten each turn it is active. Additionally, at the end of each of the creature’s turns that it spends in the radius, regardless of whether it made the dodge or not, they take an additional 9 (2d8) shock damage but do not have to make a Fortitude interaction against the Stunned effect.  
**Restoration:** When using your *Shield Recharge*, you can remove up to two effects from the target creature, other than the wounded and unconscious effects. When you choose this ability, you gain an additional use of *Shield Recharge* per downtime.  
**Swift Action:** Twice per downtime, as a Secondary Operation, you can hack into the armor of an ally within 20 feet and grant them greater freedom of movement. The chosen ally gains an additional \+10 movement speed and an additional \+15 to all dodges for up to one minute.

### Field Marshal
**Hearty:** Add \+20 to base health.  
**Improved Shields:** Add \+15 to base shields.  
**Extended Grenade Belt:** Add \+2 grenade slots.  
**Extended Hip Holster:** Add \+2 gun slots.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+15 Interaction Points.

### Lieutenant General
**Tripwire:** You have a tripwire that you can place as a Primary Operation, creating a line up to 20 feet long that requires a DC 85 detection check for someone who isn’t aware of its presence to find. Anyone who walks through  it must make a DC 80 dodge or be exposed to the fallen effect.  
**Immunity to Effects:** As a Tertiary Operation you may cleanse an ally from 1 effect other than wounded.  
**Tactical Advantage:** As a Reactive Operation, when an ally within 30 feet of you makes a dodge you may add a \+20 to the dodge. You must choose to do this before the dodge roll is made. 

### General Major
**Weapon Upgrade:** Once per downtime, you may select one weapon of your choice to upgrade. You may choose three of the following benefits for the upgraded weapon:  
\-Add an additional \+1 damage die.  
\-Increase the range of the weapon by half (rounded down).  
\-Add an additional \+2 Ammo Die.  
\-Increase the Dodge DC of the weapon by 10\.  
\-Make an additional attack provided that the weapon has more than one attack.  
**Vehicle Rewire:** Twice per downtime, you can use a Primary Operation to rewire a vehicle's hardware to rig it to explode at a time of your choosing. After rewiring the vehicle, you may choose to detonate the bomb anywhere between 1-5 rounds after the Operation. Until the designated time of explosion, the vehicle operates as normal.  
**The Tech Giveth and the Tech Taketh:** Your Shield Recharge ability now fully restores the shields of the ally it targets, and in addition, when you use this ability, you may choose one enemy within 30 feet of you and reduce their current shields by \-3d10.

### Commander
**Heart of a Lion:** Add \+30 to base health, and at the start of each of your turns you regain 5 health as long as you are conscious.  
**Shield of the People:** Add \+25 to your base shields, and once per downtime, you can bring yourself back to full shields as no Operation.  
**Loose Cannon:** Add \+3 grenade slots, and the range of all grenades you throw is doubled.  
**Artillery Expert:** Add \+3 gun slots, and you may now take the Change Weapons Operation at will on your turn without having to spend a Tertiary Operation.  
**Interaction Points:** You gain \+25 Interaction Points.

### Fleet Commander
**Jack of All Trades:** Select any two abilities of Fleet Commander rank or lower.  
**Diversified Tactics:** Select any ability from any class of Commander rank or lower, excluding Starting Abilities.  
**Retaliatory Surge:** As a Reactive Operation, after all of your shield health has been depleted, you release a surge of energy centered around yourself. Any target of your choice within 20 feet must succeed on a DC 85 dodge or take 28 (5d10) shock damage with a DC 65 Fortitude interaction.

### Hyper-Lethal Vector
**Overclock**: Once per downtime, you can boost the combat abilities of an ally who is within 50 feet of you beyond their normal limits for up to one minute. The effects of this boost are as follows:  
\-Double their shield health.  
\-They gain an additional Primary Operation on each of their turns.  
\-Add an additional \+2 damage die to all attacks.  
**Disarming Frequency:** 3 times per downtime, you can use a Secondary Operation to disable one creature’s weapon or vehicle up to 60 ft away for 1d4 turns by emitting a frequency from your helmet’s comlink that renders the weapon useless. While the weapon is disabled, it cannot be fired normally or be used in any way except for those that a hunk of metal could be used for. While a vehicle is disabled, the pilot of the vehicle cannot take any Operations with the vehicle and the vehicle’s speed is reduced to 0\. Flying vehicles will plummet as a result, as long as there is a source of gravity.

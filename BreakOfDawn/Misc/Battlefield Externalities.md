# Battlefield Externalities
Every battlefield has its own unpredictability. At initiative 0, a randomly determined effect will happen on the battlefield. The effect is determined by rolling a d20 and lasts until initiative 0 happens again. These effects on the table are incredibly general and it is up to the GM to properly flavor them to fit the situation. For example, they could explain everyone’s Dodge DC increasing due to a heavy arctic wind passing through. Note: it is not necessary to roll on this table every turn. The GM can pick and choose which effects happen. The potential effects are:

| D20 | Result                                                                                                             |
| :-- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | All combatants have the Dodge DC increased by 10\.                                                                 |
| 2   | Combatants within a 20 foot radius have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or take 11 (3d6) damage.                       |
| 3   | When combatants move through a specific area within a 20 foot radius, their movement speed is increased by 5 feet. |
| 4   | One random damage type deals an extra die of damage for the duration of the fight.                                 |
| 5   | One random creature heals 11 (3d6) health.                                                                         |
| 6   | One random creature takes 21 (6d6) damage.                                                                         |
| 7   | One random vehicle ceases to function and cannot be operated.                                                      |
| 8   | All terrain in a 20 foot radius becomes difficult terrain.                                                         |
| 9   | All creatures within a 20 foot radius have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or be exposed to the deafened effect.       |
| 10  | All creatures within a 20 foot radius have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or be exposed to the blinded effect.        |
| 11  | All creatures within a 20 foot radius have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or be exposed to the fallen effect.         |
| 12  | All creatures within a 20 foot radius have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or be exposed to the frightened effect.     |
| 13  | Up to three random creatures have to succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or be exposed to the grappled effect.                |
| 14  | All creatures must succeed on a Dodge DC 50 or have their current shield health be depleted by 50%.                |
| 15  | All creatures get an extra Tertiary Operation.                                                                     |
| 16  | All creatures get an extra Secondary Operation.                                                                    |
| 17  | All terrain in a 20 foot radius deals 14 (3d8) damage to any creature in it or that walks through it               |
| 18  | One random weapon ceases to function and cannot be operated.                                                       |
| 19  | One random turret ceases to function and cannot be operated.                                                       |
| 20  | All creatures get an extra Primary Operation.                                                                      |

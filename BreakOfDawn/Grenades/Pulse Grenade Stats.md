### Pulse Grenade

| **Core**                                                        | **Properties**                            | **Special Abilities** |
| --------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Dodge:** DC 35<br>**Damage:** 13 (2d12)<br>**Range:** 25 feet | **Type:** Hardlight<br>**Radius:** 5 feet | _Hardlight Pulse_     |

#### Special Abilities
**Hardlight Pulse:** When thrown, the Pulse Grenade creates a 5 ft by 5 ft space centered on the target that lasts until the start of the thrower’s next turn. When a creature enters or starts its turn in this space, it takes 13 (2d12) damage.

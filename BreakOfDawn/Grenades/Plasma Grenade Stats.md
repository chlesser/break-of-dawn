### Plasma Grenade

| **Core**                                                        | **Properties**                            | **Special Abilities** |
| --------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Dodge:** DC 50<br>**Damage:** 25 (10d4)<br>**Range:** 25 feet | **Type:** Explosive<br>**Radius:** 5 feet | _Adhesive_            |

#### Special Abilities
**Adhesive:** When throwing a Plasma Grenade you can attempt to stick it to the target. If you do so, the Dodge DC is reduced by -10, but if the target (and other targets within the radius) fail their save, they take an additional +5d4 damage.

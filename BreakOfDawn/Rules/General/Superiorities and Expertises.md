## Superiorities and Expertises
Spartans, as a result of their training, will have fields that they specialize in, granting them additional bonuses when making certain interactions. These bonuses are classified as either a Superiority (S), representing a minor bonus accrued through study and practice in a field, or Expertise (E), representing a large bonus gained from a great deal of knowledge and experience in a given field. The individual bonuses associated with Superiority and Expertise increase with the Spartan’s rank, as shown on the table below. 

Second Lieutenant: S= \+5, E= \+10  
First Lieutenant: S= \+5, E= \+10  
Captain: S= \+5, E= \+10  
Major: S= \+10, E= \+20  
Lieutenant-Colonel: S= \+10, E= \+20  
Colonel-Major: S= \+15, E= \+30  
Field Marshal: S= \+15, E= \+30  
Lieutenant-General: S= \+20, S= \+40  
General-Major: S= \+20, E= \+40  
Commander: S= \+25, E= \+50  
Fleet-Commander: S= \+25, E= \+50  
Hyper-Lethal Vector:S= \+30, E= \+60

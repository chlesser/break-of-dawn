## Combat Maneuvers
In combat, creatures are capable of making quick maneuvers in the heat of battle. During combat situations, you may forgo your Primary, Secondary or Tertiary Operations to perform one of these maneuvers to gain the upper hand in a fight. The options are as follows:

**Identify:**  
Cost: Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Identify maneuver, make a DC 50 History interaction targeting 1 creature within 60 feet. On a success, you may learn 1 piece of information that you ask of the GM within reason.

**Move:**  
Cost: Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Move maneuver, you target 1 friendly and willing creature within 30 feet and instruct them to move up to 5 feet in any direction, not counting against their movement.

**Survey:**  
Cost: Tertiary Operation  
Effect: You survey the battlefield to try to find enemies, search for weak points in structures, and other such effects. Make a Detection interaction determined by the GM. While this maneuver is primarily Detection, it can be replaced with other interactions in certain cases, such as identifying what weapon an enemy is carrying with Firepower or what a piece of technology is with Tech.

**Switch:**  
Cost: Tertiary Operation, 5 feet of Movement  
Effect: When taking the Switch maneuver, you switch places with 1 creature of medium size or smaller within 5 feet. If the creature is not willing, make a Lifting or Agility (player’s choice) contest. On a success, your places are swapped.

**Draw Fire:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation   
Effect: When taking the Draw Fire maneuver, make a Charisma contest against one creature within 30 feet. On a success, the creature turns its attention to you and attacks you on their turn provided you are within range and the creature can see you.

**Hull Pierce:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Hull Pierce maneuver, make a DC 50 Handling interaction against 1 vehicle within 60 feet. On a success your next attack against that vehicle does full damage. If your attack already deals full damage it instead deals \+2 additional damage die.  
   
**Prepare:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Prepare maneuver, you gain an additional Reactionary Operation until the start of your next turn.

**Push:**   
Cost: Secondary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Push maneuver, you move a creature within 5 feet by an amount depending on their size. Small creatures can be moved up to 15 feet, Medium creatures up to 10 feet and Large creatures up to 5 feet. Massive creatures cannot be targeted by this maneuver. If the creature is not willing, make a Lifting contest. On a success, the creature is pushed away.

**Brace:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Brace maneuver, the next attack made against you deals half damage (rounded down).

**Disarm:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Disarm maneuver, you make a Lifting contest with a creature within 5 feet. On a success, the weapon is knocked out of their hands and lands within 5 feet of them.

**Feint:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Feint maneuver, make a Stealth contest against a creature within 30 feet. On a success, the creature is exposed to the Surprised effect for your next attack. 

**Intimidate:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation   
Effect: When taking the Intimidate maneuver, make a Charisma contest against a creature within 30 feet. On a success the target is exposed to the Frightened effect until the start of your next turn. On a failure, the creature is immune to your Intimidate maneuver for 24 hours.

**Knockdown:**   
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Knockdown maneuver, make a Lifting or Agility contest (Player’s Choice) with 1 creature within 5 feet. On a failure, the creature is exposed to the Fallen effect.

**Recover:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Recover maneuver, make a Tech interaction. You recover an amount of shields equal to 10 \+ the result of the interaction roll divided by 10 (rounded down). 

**Scramble:**   
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Scramble maneuver, you don’t trigger chance attacks until the start of your next turn.

**Shelter:**  
Cost: Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Shelter maneuver, make a DC 50 Tactics interaction. On a success, you may treat any cover you are behind as 1 level higher for yourself only. Additionally this maneuver does not change the health of the cover. This effect is lost at the start of your next turn or when you move out of the cover you are sheltered behind.

**Dash:**  
Cost: Primary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Dash maneuver, double your movement speed for that turn.

**Hide:**

Effect: When taking the Hide maneuver, if you are within 5 feet of cover or anything you could reasonably hide behind or in, make a Stealth interaction. **Ready Up:**  
Cost: Primary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Ready Up maneuver, you are exposed to the Alert effect until the end of your next turn.

**Throw:**   
Cost: Primary Operation  
Effect: When making the Throw maneuver, you can throw an object weighing up to 25 pounds up to 30 feet with Dodge DC 50. On a hit, the object does damage equal to its poundage divided by 5 in d6.

**Ground Pound:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, 10 feet of Height, 20 feet of Movement  
If a creature jumps off of a platform at least 10 feet above the ground, they may use their Primary Operation to slam into an enemy within 15 feet of the platform, dealing 5d12 kinetic damage to the target and exposing them to the Fallen effect if they fail a Dodge DC 60\. Every creature within 5 feet must also make a DC 60 dodge or take half damage (rounded down) and be exposed to the Fallen effect if the dodge is failed. On a successful dodge, the creature making the Ground Pound maneuver will take an amount of fall damage dependent on how far they fell. Additionally, for every additional 5 feet higher the ground pound is made from, the creature may add an additional \+7 (1d12) kinetic damage. In addition to a Primary Operation, you must also expend 20 feet of your movement to make this attack.

**Gambit:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation  
Effect: When making the Gambit maneuver, make a DC 65 Firepower interaction. On a success, if your weapon can make 2 or more attacks you may make 1 additional attack on your turn. On a failure, your gun jams and you must use a Secondary Operation to fix your weapon and continue using it.

**Quick Footed:**   
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation  
Effect: When making the Quick Footed maneuver, make a DC 50 Tracking interaction. On a success you and all allies within 30 feet gain \+15 movement speed and can ignore difficult terrain.

**Prime:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation (Optional), Grenade  
Effect: When making the Prime maneuver, make DC 50 Demolition interaction. On a success you can throw a grenade and increase its radius by 5 feet. Additionally, you can choose 1 other Primary target of the grenade that is within 5 feet of the original Primary target. However, on a failure the grenade will explode in your hand unless you use a Tertiary Operation in addition to the Primary and Secondary Operations.

**Fortify:**   
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Fortify maneuver, roll a Fortitude interaction and divide the result by 5\. Until the start of your next turn you may subtract damage taken from each attack equal to this amount.

**Grapple:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When taking the Grapple maneuver, you target one creature within 5 feet and have a Lifting contest with them. On a failure the creature is exposed to the Grappled effect.

**Warcry:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation  
Effect: When making the Warcry maneuver, all friendly creatures within 30 feet gain \+5 to their Dodge DCs, \+5 Dodge bonus, and \+5 speed until the end of your next turn.

**All In:**  
Cost: Primary Operation, Secondary Operation, Tertiary Operation, All Movement  
Effect: When taking the All In maneuver, you expend all your movement speed towards 1 target and make all of your attacks at that 1 target. The Dodge DC of your attacks are increased by \+15, and all attacks that hit gain an extra damage die. If you reduce that creature’s health to 0, you may target another creature within range with all remaining attacks while still benefiting from these buffs.

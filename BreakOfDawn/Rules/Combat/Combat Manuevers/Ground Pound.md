### Ground Pound
Cost: Primary Operation, 10 feet of Height, 20 feet of Movement  
If a creature jumps off of a platform at least 10 feet above the ground, they may use their Primary Operation to slam into an enemy within 15 feet of the platform, dealing 5d12 kinetic damage to the target and exposing them to the Fallen effect if they fail a Dodge DC 60\. Every creature within 5 feet must also make a DC 60 dodge or take half damage (rounded down) and be exposed to the Fallen effect if the dodge is failed. On a successful dodge, the creature making the Ground Pound maneuver will take an amount of fall damage dependent on how far they fell. Additionally, for every additional 5 feet higher the ground pound is made from, the creature may add an additional \+7 (1d12) kinetic damage. In addition to a Primary Operation, you must also expend 20 feet of your movement to make this attack.

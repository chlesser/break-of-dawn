### Hydra Launcher
**Damage**: 33 (6d10)
**Dodge**: DC 25
**Size**: Massive
**Type**: Explosive
**Ammo Die**: 3d6
**Attacks**: 2
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 20 feet, (100 feet when homed in on a creature or vehicle)

**Special Abilities:**
*Homing:* You can use Primary Operation to home in on up to 2 creatures or vehicles within homing range, which makes the Dodge DC 90 for the next attack made with the Hydra Launcher against the targeted creature or vehicle.
*Detonation:* The target creature must make a dodge or take full damage, taking half damage (rounded down) on a success. Any creature within 5 feet of the detonation must make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

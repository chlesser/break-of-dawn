### Flamethrower
**Damage**: 7 (2d6)
**Dodge**: DC 40
**Size**: Large
**Type**: Thermal
**Ammo Die**: 3d6
**Attacks**: 1
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 15 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Spray:* The Flamethrower shoots flame 15 feet in front of it in a straight line. Any creature caught in this line must make a dodge or be exposed to the effects of the weapon.
*Ignite:* Any flammable objects within the range catch fire and deal an additional +3 (1d4) thermal damage when touched or interacted with.

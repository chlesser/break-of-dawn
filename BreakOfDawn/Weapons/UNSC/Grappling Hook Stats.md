If you attempt to attach to a creature instead, the creature must make a Dodge against the Dodge DC of the grappling hook. Until you use a Tertiary Operation to release the Grappling Hook neither you nor the target can be more than 40 feet apart. If traveling towards a targeted creature or object in this way brings you into melee range, you may make a single melee Punch attack against the target as a Tertiary Operation.

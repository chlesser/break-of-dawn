### Shotgun
**Damage**: 28 (5d10)
**Dodge**: DC 70
**Size**: Large
**Type**: Kinetic
**Ammo Die**: 2d12
**Attacks**: 1
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Average
**Range**: 10 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Split:* Damage from the Shotgun can be split between two creatures within 5 feet of each other at the user's choice. The second creature must also make a dodge roll against the same attack to avoid being hit. The user can decide the amount of damage each target takes after both dodge rolls have been made.
*Close-Quarters:* When making an attack with the Shotgun against a creature within 5 feet, you may add +2 additional damage die and the Dodge DC increases by +10.

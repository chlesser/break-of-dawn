### Sticky Detonator
**Damage**: 28 (5d10)
**Dodge**: DC 35
**Size**: Small
**Type**: Explosive
**Ammo Die**: 4d2
**Attacks**: 1
**Dual Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 15 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Sticky:* The projectiles of this weapon attach themselves to objects or creatures that they hit, requiring a DC 65 Lifting check to remove if they do not explode outright. If the attack misses the target creature, the projectile becomes attached to an unoccupied space up to 10 feet behind them.
*Charge Up:* If you hit a creature or object with this weapon, you can use Primary Operation to charge it up, doing more damage. For each Operation you charge it up, it will deal an additional +11 (2d10) explosive damage to a maximum of +50 (9d10) additional Explosive damage. Once it reaches this point, it explodes immediately. Additionally, you can use a Secondary Operation to detonate it anytime beforehand on your turn.
*Detonation:* Any creature within 5 feet of the detonation must make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

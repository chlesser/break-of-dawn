#### Special Abilities
**Combustion:** If a single target is hit by all 4 attacks from a Needler, the target takes an additional 5d8 explosive damage. All creatures and vehicles within 5 feet of the target must also make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

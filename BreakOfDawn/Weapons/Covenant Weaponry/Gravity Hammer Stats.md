#### Special Abilities
**Cleave:** If you reduce a creature to 0 health you can make a second creature within 5 feet make a dodge or take the remainder of the damage.

**Faltering Blow:** Any creature hit by an attack from the Gravity Hammer must make a DC 35 Agility interaction or be exposed to the Fallen effect.

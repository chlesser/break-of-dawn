#### Special Abilities
**Split:** Damage from the Mauler can be split between two creatures within 5 feet of each other at the user’s choice. The second creature must also make a dodge roll against the same attack to avoid being hit. The user can decide the amount of damage each target takes after both dodge rolls have been made.

**Close-Quarters:** When making an attack with the Mauler against a creature within 5 feet, you may add +1 additional damage die and the Dodge DC increases by +10.

### Boltshot
**Damage**: 9 (2d8)
**Dodge**: DC 55
**Size**: Small
**Type**: Hardlight
**Ammo Die**: 3d10 (When you lose an Ammo Die in this way, you also lose a dice from its charged fire mode)
**Attacks**: 2
**Dual-Wield**: Yes
**Precision Weapon**: Yes
**Called Shot Bonus**: +5
**Cover Negation**: Weak
**Range**: 30 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Alternate Fire:* The Boltshot has an alternate firing mode that can be seen in the stat block below. Switching between firing modes costs a Tertiary Operation.

### Boltshot (Alternate Fire)
**Damage**: 18 (4d8)
**Dodge**: DC 75
**Size**: Small
**Type**: Hardlight
**Ammo Die**: 3d4 (When you lose an Ammo Die in this way, you also lose a dice from its normal fire mode)
**Attacks**: 1
**Dual-Wield**: Yes
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Average
**Range**: 10 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Split:* Damage from the Boltshot can be split between two creatures within 5 feet of each other at the user's choice. The second creature must also make a dodge roll against the same attack to avoid being hit. The user can decide the amount of damage each target takes after both dodge rolls have been made.

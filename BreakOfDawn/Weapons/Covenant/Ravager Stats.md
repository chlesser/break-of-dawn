### Ravager
**Damage**: 20 (3d12)
**Dodge**: DC 40
**Size**: Large
**Type**: Plasma
**Ammo Die**: 3d10
**Attacks**: 2
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 20 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Brutal Edge:* When making a Punch melee attack, you may add an additional +7 (2d6) Kinetic damage while using the Ravager.
*Incendiary Blast:* Any creature within 5 feet of the target, including the target, must make a dodge or take 5 (1d8) Thermal damage.

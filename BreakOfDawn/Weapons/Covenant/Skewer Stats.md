### Skewer
**Damage**: 113 (2d12)
**Dodge**: DC 30
**Size**: Massive
**Type**: Kinetic
**Ammo Die**: 2d4
**Attacks**: 1
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: Yes
**Called Shot Bonus**: +10
**Cover Negation**: Solid
**Range**: 60 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Anti-Vehicular Rounds:* Deal full damage to vehicles.
*Brutal Edge:* When making a Punch melee attack, you may add an additional +7 (2d6) Kinetic damage while using the Skewer.

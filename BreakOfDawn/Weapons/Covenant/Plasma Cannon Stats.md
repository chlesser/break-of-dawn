### Plasma Cannon
**Damage**: 39 (7d10)
**Dodge**: DC 35
**Size**: Massive
**Type**: Explosive
**Ammo Die**: 5d2
**Attacks**: 2
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 50 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Homing:* You can use a Primary Operation to home in with the Plasma Cannon on any creature or vehicle within range, which makes the Dodge DC 85 for the next attack made with the Plasma Cannon targeting that creature.
*Detonation:* The target creature must make a dodge or take full damage, taking half damage (rounded down) on a success. Any creature within 5 feet of the detonation must make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

### Splinter Turret
**Damage**: 22 (4d10)
**Dodge**: DC 50
**Size**: Massive
**Type**: Explosive
**Ammo Die**: 5d6
**Attacks**: 2
**Dual-Wield**: No
**Precision Weapon**: No
**Called Shot Bonus**: N/A
**Cover Negation**: Flimsy
**Range**: 40 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Detonation:* The target creature must make a dodge or take full damage, taking half damage (rounded down) on a success. Any creature within 5 feet of the detonation must make a dodge or take half damage (rounded down), taking no damage on a success.

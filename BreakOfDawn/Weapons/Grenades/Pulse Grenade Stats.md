### Pulse Grenade
**Damage**: 13 (2d12)
**Dodge**: DC 35
**Type**: Hardlight
**Radius**: 5 feet
**Range**: 25 feet

**Special Abilities:**
For the next 2 rounds, the Pulse Grenade creates a 5 ft by 5 ft space centered on the target. When a creature enters or starts its turn in this space, it takes 13 (2d12) damage.

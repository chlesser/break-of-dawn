## Grenades
### Grenade Statistics
As a Primary Operation, you may arm and throw a grenade. Many of the grenades have different uses and can be applied to a variety of different scenarios and situations. The statistics of grenades are as follows:
  
**Damage:** The amount of damage each grenade deals.  
**Dodge:** The Dodge DC of the grenade.  
**Type:** The damage type of the grenade.  
**Radius:** The size of the grenade’s radius.  
**Range:** How far the grenade can be thrown without penalty.  
**Special Abilities:** Any abilities the grenade has that aren’t universal among all grenades.

#### Radius  
The size of the radius determines how many creatures or objects can be hit by a grenade. When you use a grenade, you may select one creature as the target. On a failed dodge, that target takes the full damage of the grenade, taking only half damage (rounded down) on a success. Any other creatures within the grenade’s radius must also make the dodge, taking half damage (rounded down) on a failed dodge and no damage on a success. 

#### Throwing Beyond Range  
When attempting to throw a grenade beyond its range, the Dodge DC of the grenade decreases by 5 for every 5 feet beyond the grenade’s range that it is being thrown.

#### In-Hand Detonation  
If you choose to detonate a grenade while you are within 5 feet of the target, the Dodge DC of the grenade increases by 35, but you are exposed to the grenade’s blast as well, taking half damage (rounded down) on a failed dodge and no damage on a success.

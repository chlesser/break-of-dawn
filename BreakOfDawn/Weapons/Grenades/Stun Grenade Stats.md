### Stun Grenade
**Damage**: 7 (2d6)
**Dodge**: DC 50
**Type**: Shock (Fortitude DC 50)
**Radius**: 10 feet
**Range**: 20 feet

**Special Abilities:**
*Immobilize:* Any vehicles that fail the dodge lose all movement speed and pilot Operations, however any turrets and passenger functions remain unaffected. This lasts until the end of the user's next turn.

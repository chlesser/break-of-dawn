### Gauss Turret

#### Tier I Attachment
**Improved Function**: Add +2 Ammo Die and +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Barrage**: Deals +3d10 damage to vehicles.
**Movement Modification**: Once per turn when the target is hit you may choose to push them back up to 10 feet or reduce their movement speed by 10 feet.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Rapid Fire**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Impact Rounds**: Add +1d10 damage to any damage dealt.
**Laser Sights**: Add +10 Dodge DC.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Explosive Intent**: Upon killing a target you can choose to force the target to explode. All enemies within 5 feet must succeed a Dodge DC 75, and you and all allies must succeed a Dodge DC 45. On a failure enemies take 4d10, and on a success they take half. Allies take 2d10 on a failure and no damage on a success.
**Penetration**: The Gauss Turret now has a Strong cover negation and any creature directly behind your target must also make a dodge equal to the Gauss turret's Dodge DC -10 or also take the same amount of damage as the target creature.

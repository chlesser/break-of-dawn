## Turret Attachments
Turret attachments are more powerful than standard gun attachments. Due to this, there are only three tiers of turret attachments. 

### Recommended Ranks For Gun Attachments  
These are the recommended ranks at which Spartans should receive different tiers of gun attachments. These are merely suggested and can be changed as the GM desires.

Tier 1: Major (Equivalent to a tier 2 gun attachment)  
Tier 2: Lieutenant General (Equivalent to a tier 4 gun attachment)  
Tier 3: Hyper-Lethal Vector (Equivalent to a tier 6 gun attachment)

### Rocket Turret

#### Tier I Attachment
**Improved Function**: Add +2 Ammo Die and +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Barrage**: Deals +2d20 damage to structures and vehicles.
**Movement Modification**: Once per turn when the target is hit choose to move them back 10 feet or reduce their movement speed by 10 feet.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Rapid Fire**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Damage Buff**: Add +1d20 damage to any damage dealt.
**Dodge DC Buff**: Add +10 Dodge DC.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Splinter Shot**: Increase the Detonation radius of the Rocket Turret to 10 feet.
**Homing Shots**: You may choose to add +15 Dodge DC and take a -1d20 penalty to damage.

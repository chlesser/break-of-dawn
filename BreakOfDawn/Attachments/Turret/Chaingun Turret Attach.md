### Chaingun Turret

#### Tier I Attachment
**Improved Function**: Add +2 Ammo Die and +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Barrage**: Deals the normal amount of damage to vehicles.
**Movement Modification**: Once per turn when the target is hit choose to move them back 10 feet or reduce their movement speed by 10 feet.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Rapid Fire**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Impact Rounds**: Add +1d8 damage to any damage dealt.
**Laser Sights**: Add +10 Dodge DC.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Rotating Barrel**: If you spend a Primary Operation you can rev up your barrel. As long as you shoot your chaingun turret on every subsequent turn you deal +2d8, gain a +5 to Dodge DC, and gain one additional attack.
**Flamethrower Underbarrel**: As you fire your weapon, the heat from the barrel is stored in a capsule underneath the chaingun turret. As a Primary Operation you may unleash the stored heat as flame in a 20 foot line. Any creature within that line must make a Dodge DC 60 or take an amount of Thermal Damage equal to the amount rolled on however many ammo dice you have expended, with a maximum of 3d20.

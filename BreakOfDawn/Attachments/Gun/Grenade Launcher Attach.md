### Grenade Launcher

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Rifling**: Add +5 feet of range.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Improved Gunpowder**: Add +5 radius.
**Improved Explosives**: Add +7 (1d12) to all damage.
**Destructive Detonation**: Add +13 (2d12) to all damage dealt to vehicles.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Speedy Reload**: You can reload the Grenade Launcher using no Operation.
**Incendiary Rounds**: Creatures that take damage from a Grenade Launcher's attack take an additional +4 (1d6) Thermal Damage.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Shrapnel**: Any creatures within 5 feet of the target take full damage on a failed dodge and half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Sticky Rounds**: The projectiles of this weapon attach themselves to objects or creatures that they hit, requiring a DC 70 Lifting check to remove if they do not explode outright. If the attack misses the target creature, the projectile becomes attached to an unoccupied space up to 10 feet behind them. If you hit a creature or object with this weapon, you can use Primary Operation to charge it up, doing more damage. For each Operation you charge it up, it will deal an additional +13 (2d12) explosive damage to a maximum of +113 (2d12) explosive damage. Once it reaches this point, it explodes immediately. Additionally, you can use a Secondary Operation to detonate it anytime beforehand on your turn.

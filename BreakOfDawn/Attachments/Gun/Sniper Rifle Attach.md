### Sniper Rifle

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +20 feet of range.
**Adjustable Scope**: You can reliably and accurately see up to 3 miles while looking through your scope.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Rounds**: Add +6 (1d10) damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Rounds**: Add +7 (2d6) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Rounds**: Add +7 (2d6) to any damage dealt against health.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Net Launcher**: As a Primary Operation you can fire a net out of a mounted launcher on your weapon. The target must succeed on a Dodge DC 60 or be exposed to the Incapacitated effect. The net has 20 health and is broken once enough damage is dealt to it. A broken net requires 5 minutes to repair.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Wounding Shot**: Any creature that takes 40 or more damage from a single shot of the Sniper Rifle is exposed to the Wounded effect for 2 rounds or until they regain any health.
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Solid.

#### Tier VI Attachments
**Stabilized Mount**: You may take a Primary Operation to set up your sniper's mount in a stationary position where you gain the following benefits while set up in this manner:
- Add an extra +11 (3d6) damage die on a headshot
- Dodge DC increases by 5
- Add +10 range

You lose all of these bonuses if you move and take the sniper rifle with you, in which case it requires a Primary Operation to set up again.

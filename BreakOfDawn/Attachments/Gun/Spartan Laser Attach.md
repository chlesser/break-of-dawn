### Spartan Laser

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Energy Capacity**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Rifling**: Add +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Shot**: Add +2d20 to all damage dealt to structures.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Stunning Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 50 or more they are exposed to the Stunned effect until the end of their next turn.
**Deafening Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 40 or more they are exposed to the Deafened effect until the end of their next turn.
**Blinding Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 40 or more they are exposed to the Blinded effect until the end of their next turn.
**Improved Blast**: Add +2d20 to all damage dealt.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Increased Barrel Diameter**: Add +20 Dodge DC when attacking vehicles.
**Hull-Piercing Energy**: Add +4d20 damage to vehicles, but subtract -2d20 damage from all other damage.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Fast Charge**: Instead of making one attack, you can make two more rapid attacks, each with Dodge DC 50 and dealing 53 (5d20) explosive damage.
**Shockwave**: The target must make a Dodge DC 65 or be exposed to the Fallen effect. Any creature within 5 feet of the target must make a DC 45 dodge or be exposed to the Fallen effect.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Penetrating Energy**: Any creature directly behind another creature hit by the Spartan Laser must make a dodge equal to the Dodge DC of the Spartan Laser -10 for each creature already hit or take damage equal to the damage dealt by the Spartan Laser -2 damage die for each creature already hit. The penetration ends when there are no more creatures within range or when potential damage reaches 0.
**Focusing Lens**: As a Secondary Operation you may adjust a focusing lens in front of the weapon that changes the width of the beam fired. Starting at the weapon's Dodge DC, for every 5 you increase the Dodge DC you lose -2d20 damage, and for every 5 you decrease the Dodge DC by you add +2d20 damage

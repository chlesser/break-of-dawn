### Flamethrower

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Cartridge**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Ignition**: Add +5 feet of range.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Improved Flame**: Add +4 (1d6) to all damage dealt.
**Blinding Flame**: If the target fails the dodge by 30 or more they are exposed to the Blinded effect until the end of their next turn.
**Debilitating Flame**: Any creature that takes maximum damage from the Flamethrower is exposed to the Wounded effect until they regain health in any way.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Speedy Reload**: You can reload the Flamethrower using no Operation.
**Empowered Flame**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Flame Canister**: Cover Negation increases to Weak.
**Hair Trigger**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Combustion Chamber**: When making an attack with the Flamethrower, any creature within 5 feet takes an additional +11 (3d6) Explosive damage on a failed dodge.

### Bulldog

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 2 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +2 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +10 feet of range.
**Blunted Bullets**: When hit by an attack, targets within 10 feet are pushed back up to 10 feet if they are size medium or smaller, or up to 5 feet if they are size large.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Rounds**: Add +7 (1d12) damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Rounds**: Add +9 (2d8) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Rounds**: Add +9 (2d8) to any damage dealt against health.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Plasma Shield**: A portable energy shield is encased within the Bulldog with 50 shield health and can be activated and deactivated as a Secondary Operation. When you fail a dodge by 20 or less while the shield is active, the damage is transferred to the shield. While deactivated, the shield regains 10 shield health at the start of each of your turns.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Hair Trigger**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Average.

#### Tier VI Attachments
**Enlarged Barrel**: The Bulldog adds +15 to called shots when within 10 feet of the target.
**Compact Stock**: The Bulldog is now small size and obtains the dual wielding trait.

### Sticky Detonator

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +5 feet of range.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Improved Gunpowder**: Add +5 radius.
**Improved Explosives**: Add +6 (1d10) to all damage.
**Destructive Detonation**: Add +11 (2d10) to all damage dealt to vehicles.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Speedy Reload**: You can reload the Sticky Detonator using no Operation.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Shrapnel**: Any creatures within 5 feet of the target take full damage on a failed dodge and half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Airborne Charge**: Any creature stuck with the Sticky Detonator must make an additional DC 50 dodge, or take the maximum damage die allowed by a fully charged blast, provided it hasn't already detonated.

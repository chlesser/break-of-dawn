## Gun Attachments
Along the way, your fireteam may find or craft attachments for their weapons or armor that grant them new abilities or make existing ones even better. However, given that the UNSC does not know how Promethean or Covenant weapons function, they can only craft attachments for themselves. Attachments can be found through certain areas on Earth and her colonies, as well as on battlefields and possibly dead bodies. Each weapon can only have one attachment of each Tier at a given time, but can be swapped out at any time using a Primary Operation.

### Recommended Ranks For Gun Attachments
These are the recommended ranks at which Spartans should receive different tiers of gun attachments. These are merely suggested and can be changed as the GM desires.

**Tier 1**: 1st Lieutenant
**Tier 2**: Major
**Tier 3**: Colonel Major
**Tier 4**: Lieutenant General
**Tier 5**: Commander
**Tier 6**: Hyper-Lethal Vector

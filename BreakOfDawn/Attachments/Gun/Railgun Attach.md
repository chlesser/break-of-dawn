### Railgun

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Rifling**: Add +10 feet of range.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Stunning Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 50 or more they are exposed to the Stunned effect until the start of their next turn.
**Deafening Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 40 or more they are exposed to the Deafened effect until the end of their next turn.
**Blinding Blast**: If the target fails the dodge by 40 or more they are exposed to the Blinded effect until the end of their next turn.
**Improved Blast**: Add +11 (1d20) to all damage dealt.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Homing Chip**: Increase the Dodge DC by +25 when attacking vehicles.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Wounding Shot**: Any creature who takes 40 or more damage from a single attack is exposed to the Wounded effect for 2 rounds or until they regain any health.
**Electric Charge**: Any creatures within 5 feet of a creature hit by the Railgun must make a DC 50 dodge or take half of the damage (rounded down).
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Strong.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Penetrating Shells**: Any creature directly behind another creature hit by the Railgun must make a dodge equal to the Dodge DC of the Railgun -10 for each creature already hit or take damage equal to the damage dealt by the Railgun -1 damage die for each creature already hit. The penetration ends when there are no more creatures within range or when potential damage reaches 0.

### Hydra Launcher

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extra Barrel**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Rifling**: Add +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Shot**: Add +2d20 to all damage dealt to structures.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Explosives**: Add +6 (1d10) damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Explosives**: Add +7 (1d12) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Explosives**: Add +7 (1d12) to any damage dealt against health.
**Destructive Detonation**: Add +11 (2d10) to all damage dealt to vehicles.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Speedy Reload**: You can reload the Hydra Launcher using no Operation.
**Tracking Homing Chip**: When Homing, add +25 Dodge DC when attacking vehicles.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Add +10 Dodge DC.
**Blinding Fire**: Any creature who fails the dodge by 40 or more is exposed to the Blinded effect until the end of their next turn
**Deafening Wave**: Any creature who fails the dodge by 40 or more is exposed to the Deafened effect until the end of their next turn.
**Shrapnel Explosives**: Any creature within 5 feet of the target must make a Dodge DC 40 or take 3d10 kinetic damage.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Wounding Explosives**: Any creature that takes 45 or more damage in a single attack is exposed to the Wounded effect for two rounds or until they regain any health.
**Fast Charge**: While homing in on a target, you may spend a Secondary Operation to home in on a second target.

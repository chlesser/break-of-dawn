### DMR (Designated Marksman Rifle)

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 2 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +2 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +20 feet of range.
**Adjustable Scope**: You can reliably and accurately see up to a mile while looking through your scope.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Rounds**: Add +7 (1d12) damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Rounds**: Add +9 (2d8) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Rounds**: Add +9 (2d8) to any damage dealt against health.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Bayonet**: In exchange of a single attack with this weapon, you can make a melee attack against a creature within 5 feet dealing 18 (4d8) kinetic damage using the same Dodge DC as the weapon.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Hair Trigger**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Sturdy.

#### Tier VI Attachments
**Compact Stock**: The DMR is now small size and obtains the dual wielding trait.
**Sturdier Firing Pin**: Called shots deal an additional +11 (2d10) damage.

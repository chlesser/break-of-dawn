### Rocket Launcher

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extra Barrel**: You may reroll 1 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +1 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation

#### Tier II Attachment
**Improved Rifling**: Add +10 feet of range.
**Crumbling Shot**: Add +2d20 to all damage dealt to structures.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Improved Gunpowder**: Add +5 radius.
**Improved Explosives**: Add +11 (1d20) to all damage.
**Destructive Detonation**: Add +2d20 to all damage dealt to vehicles.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Speedy Reload**: You can reload the Rocket Launcher using no Operation.
**Homing Chip**: Add +25 Dodge DC when attacking vehicles.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Shrapnel**: Any creatures within 5 feet of the target take full damage on a failed dodge and half damage (rounded down) on a successful dodge.
**Blinding Fire**: Any creature who fails the dodge by 35 or more is exposed to the Blinded effect until the end of their next turn.
**Deafening Wave**: Any creature who fails the dodge by 35 or more is exposed to the Deafened effect until the end of their next turn.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Wounding Explosives**: Any creature that takes 45 or more damage inside the radius is exposed to the Wounded effect for 2 rounds or until they next regain health.
**Split Explosives**: Instead of firing normally, you can turn the shell into 3 smaller tracking missiles that are treated as 3 separate attacks, each dealing 2d20 damage with Dodge DC 75.

### Battle Rifle

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 3 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +3 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +15 feet of range

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Rounds**: Add +5 (1d8) damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Rounds**: Add +6 (1d10) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Rounds**: Add +6 (1d10) to any damage dealt against health.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Bayonet**: In exchange for a single attack with this weapon, you can make a melee attack against a creature within 5 feet dealing 18 (4d8) kinetic damage using the same Dodge DC as the weapon.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Hair Trigger**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Average.

#### Tier VI Attachments
**Compact Stock**: The Battle Rifle is now small size and obtains the dual wielding trait.
**Bullet Tracker**: If your first shot hits a creature, the Dodge DC increases by 20 for any other attacks made against them during that turn.

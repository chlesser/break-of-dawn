### SMG (Submachine Gun)

#### Tier I Attachment
**Extended Magazine**: You may reroll 5 Ammo Die per downtime.
**Improved Storage**: Add +5 Ammo Die.
**Flashlight**: Sheds bright light in a 15 foot cone and can be activated or deactivated as a Tertiary Operation.

#### Tier II Attachment
**Barrel Stabilizer**: Add +10 feet of range.

#### Tier III Attachment
**Impact Rounds**: Add +1d2 damage to any damage dealt.
**Armor Piercing Rounds**: Add +3 (1d4) to any damage dealt against shields.
**High Caliber Rounds**: Add +3 (1d4) to any damage dealt against health.

#### Tier IV Attachment
**Laser Sights**: Increase the Dodge DC by +10.
**Customized Holster**: The SMG now costs 0 gun slots.

#### Tier V Attachment
**Hair Trigger**: You may make an additional attack with this weapon.
**Penetrant Rounds**: Cover Negation increases to Weak.

#### Tier VI Attachment
**Textured Grip**: You can both switch to the SMG and reload it using no Operation, and when being dual wielded, you may make one extra attack with the SMG as a second weapon.
